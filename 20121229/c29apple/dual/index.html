苹果CEO降薪了？其实未必

A Pay Cut for Tim Cook? Not Really

JENNA WORTHAM
2012年12月29日
This year, Apple became the most highly valued public company in history and generated $156.5 billion in revenue — nearly 50 percent more than in 2011. So how much did Timothy D. Cook, the company’s chief executive, take home?
今年，苹果(Apple)成了史上市值最高的上市公司，创造了1565亿美元（约合9760亿元人民币）的收入，同2011年相比，增幅接近50%。那么，苹果公司的首席执行官蒂莫西·D·库克(Timothy D. Cook)的酬劳是多少呢？
A relatively modest $4.2 million in pay, according to documents filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on Thursday. The filing, made in advance of the company’s annual shareholder meeting on Feb. 27, revealed that Mr. Cook had earned a salary of $1.36 million and received a cash bonus of $2.8 million.
根据周四提交给美国证券交易委员会(Securities and Exchange Commission)的文件显示，库克获得的年收入为420万美元，相对而言不算太多。明年2月27日，苹果将召开年度股东会议，在会议之前公布的这些文件显示，库克的工资所得为136万美元，外加280万美元的现金奖金。
That may seem to be a steep decline from the $378 million package that Mr. Cook was awarded in 2011, one of the largest on record, bolstered by a stock package that was worth $376.2 million at the time.
同2011年3.78亿美元的薪酬相比，库克的报酬似乎大幅缩水了。他当年的收入也是有史以来金额最高的一次，其中他持有的股票当时价值3.762亿美元。
But the stock will vest over a 10-year period. Frank Gillett, an analyst with Forrester Research, said that this move was in line with Apple’s overall strategy, from its product development plans to its long-term goals for the executive at the helm.“If you want to create success for the company, you want to create it based on the long-term success of the company,” he said. “You put a big carrot on the horizon, about 10 years out, and go from there.”
但他持有的股票属于10年期的长期投资。弗雷斯特研究公司(Forrester Research)的分析师弗兰克·吉勒特(Frank Gillett)称，此举同苹果的总体战略是一致的，从公司的产品研发计划，到高管的长远目标，都包括在苹果的总体战略中。“如果你想为公司创造成功，你就要把这种成功建立在公司的长远成功上，”他说。“你把一个大胡萝卜放在将来大约需要10年时间才能到达的地方，以此为起点。”
Mr. Gillett also said that although Mr. Cook had not received any additional stock benefits this year, the board had increased his salary to $1.36 million from $900,000. He also pointed out that for a period of time, the company’s former chief executive and founder, Steven P. Jobs, had earned a salary of just one dollar.
吉勒特也表示，尽管库克今年没有得到任何额外的股票收益，但董事会已经将他的工资从90万美元提高到了136万美元。他还指出，有一段时间，苹果公司的前任首席执行官兼创始人史蒂文·P·乔布斯(Steven P. Jobs)的工资只有一美元。
“If you were handing out gigantic nine-figure stock deals every year, it would send the wrong message to customers and shareholders,” he said.
“如果你每年都发放九位数的高额股票分红，这会给消费者和股东传达出错误的信息，”他说。
