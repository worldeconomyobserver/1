詹姆斯·卡梅隆落子天津

China Draws In an A-List Hollywood Director

JONATHAN LANDRETH
2012年8月10日
BEIJING — James Cameron, director of the global hit films “Titanic” and “Avatar,” said Wednesday he was setting up shop in the Chinese city of Tianjin to bring his cutting-edge 3-D technologies and production services to a high-technology industrial park aimed at aiding China’s booming moviemaking industry.
北京——周三，执导过全球热卖电影《泰坦尼克》和《阿凡达》的导演詹姆斯·卡梅隆(James Cameron)表示，他将在中国港口城市天津安营扎寨，以将其尖端的3D技术和电影制作服务引入当地的一个高科技产业园区，其目的是助力中国欣欣向荣的电影制作行业。
Mr. Cameron and his partner, Vince Pace, were in the Chinese port city of 13 million, a 30-minute train ride from the capital, Tuesday to cut the ribbon on the new office of Cameron Pace Group China, a joint venture with Tianjin North Film Group and the High-Tech Holding Group, both state-owned entities.
周二，卡梅隆和他的合作伙伴文斯·佩斯(Vince Pace)来到了天津，并出席了卡梅隆-佩斯集团中国公司(Cameron Pace Group China，简称CPG 中国)新办公室的揭幕仪式。CPG中国是由卡梅隆与天津北方电影集团(Tianjin North Film Group)和高新控股集团(High-Tech Holding Group)共同成立的一家合资企业。这两家中方合作伙伴都是国有企业。天津拥有1300万人口，从北京坐火车只需30分钟便可抵达。
C.P.G. China will try to bring the latest in 3-D filmmaking tools to Chinese producers and directors who are trying to wow the nation’s swelling and entertainment-hungry middle class. About $2.1 billion worth of movie tickets were sold last year, up 30 percent from 2010.
CPG中国将努力把最新的3D电影制作工具带给寻求吸引中产阶级的中国电影制作人和导演。中国的中产阶级规模不断壮大，对娱乐有着热切的渴求。去年，中国的电影票房收入约为21亿美元，同2010年相比增幅达30%。
Mr. Cameron called Wang Dafang, the chairman of Tianjin North Film Group, “a very passionate, visionary guy” who drew C.P.G. into Tianjin over other potential partners in Beijing and Shanghai.
卡梅隆称，天津北方电影集团董事长王大方 “是一个非常有热情、有远见的人”。正是王大方促成了CPG中国放弃北京和上海的其他潜在合作伙伴，最终落户天津。
“Tianjin felt like the right place for us because of their emphasis on high technology and new development and rapid economic growth,” Mr. Cameron said Wednesday. “It’s almost a feedback loop. Their vision for the future feeds their success and their success feeds their vision.”
“天津感觉就是我们应该来的地方，因为他们非常重视高科技、新发展和经济的快速增长，”卡梅隆在周三表示。“这几乎是一个良性循环。他们对未来的构想促进了他们的成功，而成功又强化了他们对外来的构想。”
Mr. Cameron added that the high-technology area of Tianjin where C.P.G. China was established posted economic growth of roughly 25 percent last year, well above the overall city’s rise of 16 percent.
他补充说， 天津高新科技区去年经济增速约为25%，远远高于整个天津市16%的平均增长水平。
In the first quarter, Chinese ticket sales surpassed Japan’s for the first time, making China into Hollywood’s largest export box office market. In spite of the government’s limit on film imports — only 34 are allowed to share in the box office take each year — Hollywood had 8 of the top 10 places on the box office ranking list from January through June, led by Mr. Cameron’s “Titanic 3-D.”
今年第一季度，中国电影票房收入首次超过日本，中国由此成为好莱坞最大的出口票房市场。尽管中国政府对外国电影做出限制，每年仅允许引进34部外国电影，但在1至6月的票房收入排行榜上，排名前十的电影中，有八部是好莱坞影片，其中的领衔之作便是卡梅隆的3D版《泰坦尼克号》。
In a testament to his popularity with Chinese moviegoers and the power of 3-D to draw consumers willing to pay a premium, the 3-D remake of the 1997 hit grossed 915 million renminbi, or $144 million, at the box office. That was $41 million more than the runner-up, “Mission Impossible: Ghost Protocol.”
《泰坦尼克号》是卡梅隆1997年的大作。重新制作后的3D版在中国的票房收入为9.15亿元，约合1.44亿美元，比位列其后的《碟中谍：幽灵协议》高出4100万美元。这充分证明了卡梅隆在中国电影观众当中的受欢迎程度以及3D技术在吸引消费者购买高价电影票方面的能力。
In the coming years, China’s movie ticket sales are projected to surpass U.S. ticket sales of nearly $11 billion in 2011, industry estimates show, and Mr. Cameron said he hoped to be a part of the growth of the Chinese market.
行业预测显示，中国的电影票房市场将有望超过美国2011年的市场规模，即近110亿美元。卡梅隆说希望自己能为中国电影市场的增长贡献自己的力量。

翻译：陈亦亭
