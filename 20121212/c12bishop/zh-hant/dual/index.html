習近平讓人耳目一新

In China, New Leadership and New Style

利明璋
2012年12月12日
Xi Jinping visited Shenzhen over the weekend in a move seen by some as a sign of his commitment to further economic reforms. The choice of this city as the destination for his first domestic trip as general secretary is seen as a strong signal that Mr. Xi is hitching himself to Deng Xiaoping's legacy and style and is serious about reinvigorating reforms.
習近平上周末的深圳之行，有人認為標誌着他將致力於深化經濟改革。作為總書記，他選擇深圳作為首次國內視察的目的地，這被視為一個強烈的信號，說明習近平將繼承鄧小平的政治遺產和執政風格，並會認真對待重振改革這一議題。
Deng Xiaoping began his famous 1992 "Southern Tour" from Shenzhen to reinvigorate reform following conservative stagnation after the 1989 suppression of pro-democracy protests. On Saturday Mr. Xi placed a wreath at a statue of Mr. Deng and then went into the crowd of onlookers to shake hands.
1992年，鄧小平從深圳出發，開始了著名了“南巡”。之前，中國在1989年對民主抗議活動進行了鎮壓之後，經歷了保守的停滯時期。周六，習近平向鄧小平的銅像獻上花圈，隨後又走進圍觀的人群中和人們握手。
Mr. Xi clearly has a different political style that is welcomed by many Chinese. The new leadership is trying to shift perceptions and rebuild public confidence even though so far there have been no substantive policy changes. Xi is moving quickly to project confidence and commitment to reform, as this column predicted two months ago.
顯然，習近平與眾不同的政治風格受到很多中國人的歡迎。新一屆中國領導人正力圖改變人們的印象和重建公眾信心，儘管到目前為止還沒有出現實質性的政策變化。正如本專欄在兩個月前預計的一樣，習近平正在迅速行動，以表現他對改革的信心和決心。
Reuters reports that Chinese Internet censors loosened restrictions on some Internet search terms after Mr. Xi's visit. Blocks on overseas sites like Twitter remain and this possible fillip does not appear to include loosening restrictions on accessing the global Internet, as China's propaganda head recently called for more "research" on the strengthening of Internet controls.
路透社(Reuters)報道，在習近平視察深圳之後，中國互聯網審查者放鬆了對一些搜索關鍵詞的限制。像Twitter這樣的海外站點依然受到屏蔽。這種可能是微小放鬆的舉動似乎並不包括放寬對接入全球互聯網的限制，因為中國中宣部部長在最近呼籲，要加強互聯網控制方面的“研究”。
Internet controls are definitely more relaxed if you are exposing corruption. Several more junior and midlevel officials ran afoul of online graft busters, including a police chief in a city in Xinjiang who was accused of keeping twin sisters as mistresses and the police chief of Taiyuan, who allegedly covered up his son's arrest for driving under the influence and hitting a police officer, even after a video of his arrest went viral online.
在揭發腐敗方面，互聯網的控制確實更加放鬆了。有好幾名低層和中層官員因網絡舉報而落馬，其中包括新疆某市的公安局長和太原市的公安局長，前者被指控包養雙胞胎姐妹，後者據稱包庇其子的醉駕襲警行為，甚至在其子被捕的視頻已在網上瘋傳之後，仍試圖掩蓋。
In what appears to be the first non-anonymous accusation on the Internet about a ministerial-level official, Luo Changping (Sina Weibo account), the deputy editor of Caijing magazine, took to Sina Weibo to publicly accuse Liu Tienan, the Director of the National Energy Administration, of fudging his resume and engaging in corruption. Luo made the accusations while Mr. Liu was traveling in Russia with Wang Qishan, the new anti-corruption czar
而對一名部級官員的揭發似乎是首例互聯網非匿名舉報案例。在那次網絡爆料中，《財經》雜誌的副主編羅昌平在新浪微博上公開舉報國家能源局局長劉鐵男偽造學歷、涉嫌腐敗。在羅昌平發出這些指控之時，劉鐵男正和新上任的反腐最高官員王岐山一同出訪俄羅斯。
THE CRACKDOWN ON CORRUPTION has been previously discussed in this column. The propaganda authorities are allowing, and in fact may be encouraging, more reporting on corruption exposes but so far only one senior official-Li Chuncheng-has been detained. Expect the crackdown to last longer than most assume but not address the structural sources of graft.
本專欄之前曾討論過打擊腐敗的問題。宣傳部門允許，實際上也許是鼓勵，對腐敗曝光事件進行更多的報道，但到目前為止，只有一位高官——李春城被捕。反腐行動的持續時間可能會比大多數人預想的要長，但可能不會觸及貪污的體制根源。
The Chinese stock markets hit a one-month high on Monday, possibly in reaction to Mr. Xi's symbolic trip and some mostly positive economic data that came out over the last few days. The recent pickup in the economy, however, may be unsustainable without difficult reforms:
周一，中國股市達到了一個月來的最高點，這可能歸因於習近平具有象徵意義的深圳之行以及過去幾天出現的一些比較積極的經濟數據。然而，如果不進行艱難的改革，近期的經濟回升可能不會持續：
"The extremely weak exports performance laid bare the fragility of the recovery, which is mainly driven by infrastructure investment," said Ren Xianfang, a Beijing-based analyst with IHS Global Insight. "Xi's leadership team should accelerate reform to unleash and boost domestic demand to make growth more sustainable and less susceptible to external headwinds."
“極其疲軟的出口表現說明主要依靠基礎設施投資帶動的經濟復蘇較為脆弱，”環球通視(IHS Global Insight)中國分析師任先芳說。“習近平的領導班子應該加快改革步伐，擴大和刺激內需，促使經濟增長更具可持續性，少受外部經濟衰退的影響。”
A recent survey by Gan Li, a professor at Southwestern University of Finance and Economics and Texas A&M University, finds that China's urban unemployment rate is 8.05 percent, nearly double the official number. Professor Gan's research also claims that China's Gini coefficient, a measure of income inequality, is 0.61, far higher level at which most experts say there is risk of social unrest.
西南財經大學、德州農工大學(Texas A&M University)教授甘犁近期的一項調查發現，中國城鎮失業率為8.05%，差不多是官方數據的兩倍。甘犁的研究還發現，中國顯示收入差別的基尼係數為0.61，大多數專家認為，已經遠遠高於可能造成社會不安定的上限。
Mr. Xi clearly has his hands full, though the fact that China's urban unemployment rate and Gini coefficient are much worse than all but the most pessimistic observers have argued could be seen as an indicator that Chinese society is more resilient and the Party's grip on power is stronger than many outside observers assume. Mr. Xi does know that reforms are needed. Changing public style is relatively easy, the structural reforms required will not be.
很顯然，習近平會很忙，儘管中國城鎮失業率以及基尼係數比最悲觀的觀察人士所認為的更糟糕，本身可能就說明了，中國社會的彈性更大，共產黨對權力的掌控比外界觀察人士的猜測也更加牢固。習近平的確知道需要進行改革。改變公眾形象相對比較容易，而必要的體制改革就不會那麼輕而易舉了。
