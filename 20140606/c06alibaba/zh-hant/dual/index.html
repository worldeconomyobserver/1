阿里巴巴2億美元入股恆大足球俱樂部

Alibaba to Take Stake in Chinese Soccer Team

NEIL GOUGH
2014年6月6日
HONG KONG — The e-commerce giant Alibaba Group agreed on Thursday to pay nearly $200 million for a stake in a Chinese soccer team, its latest in a recent spate of acquisitions.
香港——周四，電子商務巨頭阿里巴巴集團同意支付近2億美元（約合12億元人民幣）入股一支中國足球隊，這是它最近展開的連串收購中最新的一筆交易。
Alibaba, which is moving forward with plans for an initial public offering in the United States that could surpass Facebook’s $16 billion listing two years ago, said it would buy a 50 percent stake in Guangzhou Evergrande F.C. from the team’s parent company, the Evergrande Real Estate Group, a property developer based in the southern Chinese city of Guangzhou and listed in Hong Kong.
即將按計劃赴美上市的阿里巴巴表示，公司將從廣州恆大俱樂部的母公司恆大地產集團手中買下恆大俱樂部50%的股份，恆大地產集團是一家地產開發公司，總部位於中國南部城市廣州，在香港上市。阿里巴巴的上市招股規模，可能會比Facebook兩年前上市時的160億美元還要大。
Alibaba’s chairman, Jack Ma, made the announcement at a news conference in Guangzhou on Thursday with Xu Jiayin, the chairman of the developer. Financial details of the deal were not initially disclosed, but a report in The China Business Journal on Thursday said that Alibaba had agreed to pay 1.2 billion renminbi, or $192 million.
周四，阿里巴巴集團董事局主席馬雲在廣州的新聞發佈會上和恆大集團主席許家印共同發佈了這一消息。雙方一開始並未披露交易的財務細節，不過，《中國經營報》周四刊登的一篇報道稱，阿里巴巴已同意支付12億元人民幣，即約1.92億美元達成交易。
Alibaba has been branching beyond its core online shopping business in recent months, investing in sectors that include online video, film production, wireless voice calling and logistics. It was not immediately clear on Thursday, however, how the investment in the soccer club would complement Alibaba’s portfolio of businesses as it woos American investors for its I.P.O.
近幾個月，阿里巴巴一直在核心網購業務之外拓展其他業務，向包括網絡視頻、電影製作、無線語音電話和物流在內的行業注入了資金。 然而，在周四消息公布時尚不能清晰看出的是，阿里巴巴為自己的首次公開募股爭取美國投資者之際，投資這家足球俱樂部對它的業務組合能形成怎樣的補充。
But several recent deals have signaled Alibaba’s push into the entertainment business. In April, Alibaba and Yunfeng Capital, a private equity company controlled by Mr. Ma, paid $1.2 billion for an 18.5 percent stake in Youku Tudou, a Chinese online video company.In March, the e-commerce company announced plans for a new alternative investment product that would crowdsource funds for film production. Also in March, Alibaba paid about $800 million for a stake in ChinaVision Media Group, a Hong Kong-traded company that invests in film, television, newspapers and multimedia businesses.
但最近的幾筆交易表明了阿里巴巴進軍娛樂業務的努力。今年4月，阿里巴巴和馬雲控制的私募股本公司雲峰基金出資12億美元，收購了中國在線視頻公司優酷馬鈴薯18.5%的股份。3月，這家電商巨頭宣布了將推出一款新的替代性投資產品的計劃，該款產品將通過「眾籌」為電影製作籌集資金。同在3月，阿里巴巴出資約8億美元入股在香港上市的文化中國傳媒集團。該公司的投資領域包括電影、電視、報紙和多媒體業務。
Guangzhou Evergrande is one of the most ambitious soccer teams in China, where the sport is characterized by scrappy and sometimes controversial play. Last year, the team won the title in the Asian Football Confederation Champions League. Evergrande has also worked with Chinese universities to popularize the sport and has invested millions of dollars in setting up its own international soccer school.
廣州恆大是中國最具雄心壯志的足球隊之一，足球這項運動在中國以雜亂無章、時有爭議性比賽而著稱。去年，恆大在亞洲足球俱樂部冠軍聯賽奪冠。恆大還和中國的大學合作，推廣足球運動，並已投資數百萬美元創辦自己的國際足球學校。
