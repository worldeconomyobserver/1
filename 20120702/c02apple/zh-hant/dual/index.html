iPad商標權案和解告終

Apple Settles an iPad Dispute in China

KEITH BRADSHER
2012年7月2日
HONG KONG — A Chinese provincial court said on Monday that Apple had settled a lawsuit there by agreeing to pay $60 million for the legal rights to use the iPad trademark in China, according to Xie Xianghui, a lawyer for the Chinese company involved.
香港—據蘋果商標權一案的中方企業代理律師謝湘輝透露，中國一家地方法院在周一宣布，蘋果已同意支付6000萬美金，從而使該公司獲得了在中國使用iPad商標的使用權。
“It was done last week and it was confirmed with a ruling by the higher court,” which was issued on Monday morning, said Mr. Xie, a lawyer at the Grandall Legal Group in Shenzhen, China, which represented Shenzhen Proview Technology.
中國深圳國浩律師事務所的謝湘輝律師是深圳唯冠公司的代理人。他表示：“案子在上周就結束了，並已得到地方高等法院核定。”和解結果在周一上午宣布。
It should take only a week or two for China’s national trademark authority to transfer the iPad trademark for China to Apple, Mr. Xie added.
謝湘輝補充說，只需一到兩周時間，中國國家工商總局下屬商標局就會把在中國註冊的iPad商標轉讓給蘋果。
Mr. Xie said that Proview Technology did not regard the settlement amount as especially large, “but it is O.K.”
他還表示，唯冠並不認為蘋果支付的和解數額特別巨大，“但還可以接受。”
Apple had been in settlement negotiations this spring with the Chinese subsidiary of Proview Technology of Taiwan in the long-running trademark dispute. Apple did not immediately respond on Monday morning to requests for comment.
今年春季，蘋果和台灣唯冠的大陸子公司一直在就和解方案進行談判，探討如何解決久拖不決的商標爭議。周一，蘋果沒有就立刻回應記者的置評要求。
According to testimony in the legal fight, Apple retained British lawyers several years ago to set up a company, IP Application Development, to buy up rights to the iPad name around the world. Apple paid only £35,000 to Proview Technology for the Taiwanese company’s iPad trademarks in various countries.
根據這場法律糾紛中涉案雙方的證詞，蘋果在幾年之前僱傭了英國律師，並通過他們設立了一家 名為IP Application Development的公司，並通過該公司在全球買斷iPad的商標權。蘋果僅向台灣唯冠支付了3.5萬英鎊即購得該公司在幾個國家地區註冊的iPad 商標。
But when Apple introduced the iPad with immediate success, Proview Technology said that the transaction had not included the trademark for China, which was held by Shenzhen Proview Technology, its mainland Chinese subsidiary. The Shenzhen subsidiary, located in Guangdong province in southeastern China, filed a trademark infringement case against Apple in the Guangdong provincial court
但當iPad初一問世即大獲成功的時候，唯冠表示，由於iPad在中國大陸的商標權為其子公司深圳唯冠所有，所以當年的商標權交易並不包括該商標在中國大陸的使用權。隨後，位於中國東南部廣東省的唯冠深圳子公司向廣東省法院提起了針對蘋果的商標侵權訴訟。
The Guangdong High People’s Court in Guangzhou, a metropolis of 12 million people that is the provincial capital, said on Monday that the two sides had reached the settlement and that this would end the litigation. China has emerged as one of Apple’s largest markets.
周一，在擁有1200萬人口的廣東省首府廣州市，該省高級人民法院宣布雙方已經達成和解，訴訟自動中止。目前，中國已成為蘋果產品全球最大的市場之一。
