中石化董事長談中國油企國際化

Chinese Oil Executive Learning From Experience

STANLEY REED
2012年11月14日
LONDON — Holding court in a suite at the swank Dorchester Hotel here, Fu Chengyu, the chairman of Sinopec, the giant state oil and petrochemical company in China, enjoys talking about his business. Aides try to hurry him along, but he gives detailed, enthusiastic answers to questions.
倫敦——在豪華的都切斯特酒店(Dorchester Hotel)套房裡，中國國有石油石化行業巨頭，中國石油化工集團公司董事長傅成玉接待了來訪的記者。他很樂於暢談他的企業。儘管助手們一再催促，他依然對提問給出了詳盡而激情洋溢的回答。 
During his career, Mr. Fu has helped lead the Chinese energy industry’s efforts to modernize and internationalize. In recent years, China, which is a major importer of oil and natural gas, has been trying to lock up energy supplies overseas to feed its economy.
在他的職業生涯中，傅成玉曾帶動中國能源業走向行業現代化和國際化。近些年，作為世界主要的石油和天然氣進口國，中國一直試圖鎖定海外能源供應，以滿足本國經濟需求。
Sometimes this drive led to bitter disappointments, most notably when political opposition in the United States forced him to give up his proposed acquisition of Unocal, a midsize U.S. oil company, in 2005. Still, Mr. Fu himself receives high marks from people who have met him.
有時，這種努力的結果是痛苦的失望。最引人注目的事件發生在2005年，當時，美國政治上的反對派迫使他放棄了收購美國一家中型石油公司優尼科(Unocal)的交易。儘管如此，見過傅成玉的人對他都讚賞有加。
“Fu Chengyu has long been the most entrepreneurial and strategic thinker of the Chinese N.O.C. leaders,” David L. Goldwyn, a former state department international energy envoy, said, using the term for national oil companies. “He has long sought to access both capital and technology through partnership, and unique among his peers, he sees partnership as a two-way street, and a long-term, rather than transitional, effort.”
美國國務院前國際能源事務特使戴維·L·戈爾德溫(David L. Goldwyn)說，“傅成玉一直是中國國家石油公司中最具企業領袖才能的戰略思想家。長期以來，他一直尋求通過建立夥伴關係獲得資本和技術。他與眾不同之處在於，能把夥伴關係視作一種雙向的交流，一種長期的關係，而不是過渡性的。”
Mr. Fu is in London to speak Tuesday at the Oil and Money Conference convened by the International Herald Tribune and Energy Intelligence Group, a provider of independent analysis and data to the global energy industry.
傅成玉這次來倫敦是為了在周二的石油和貨幣會議(Oil and Money Conference)上發表講話。這個大會是《國際先驅論壇報》(International Herald Tribune)和能源情報集團(Energy Intelligence Group)共同舉辦的 ，後者是一個為全球能源行業提供獨立分析和數據的機構。
Mr. Fu appears to be a man who does not mince words and who learns from mistakes.
傅成玉似乎是一個直言不諱、善於從錯誤中吸取教訓的人。
Mr. Fu says that the Unocal failure led him to rethink his foreign investment strategy. He says he now goes for partnerships in the United States, like one that the company has with Devon Energy, of Houston, for shale gas and oil rather than outright acquisitions.
傅成玉說，收購優尼科失敗讓他重新思考他的對外投資戰略。他說，他正在美國尋求合作夥伴。他的企業採取合作方式和休斯敦的戴文能源公司(Devon Energy)開發頁岩氣和石油，而不是直接收購。
“The investor must make the local people and government feel good,” he said in English. “Like it or not, good or bad. That is the way you have to take it.”
他用英語說，“不管你喜不喜歡，是好還是壞，投資人都必須讓當地人和政府感覺良好。這是你必須採取的方式。”
He said that Chinese oil companies still had ground to cover to make themselves better understood by investors, potential host countries and partners.
他說，中國石油企業依然有很多地方需要改進，以便讓投資人、潛在的投資國和合作夥伴更好地了解它們。
“I think Chinese companies have more work to do from this side, to become more transparent and more open,” he said. “I think we have become better but still there is a long way to go.”
他說，“我認為，中國企業自身有更多的工作需要做，企業需要變得更加透明和更加開放。我想，中國企業已經有所改善，但要走的路依然很長。”
The Unocal debacle, which occurred when Mr. Fu was running Cnooc, the government company charged with making international deals, looked like a career killer. But Mr. Fu survived and last year took over at Sinopec, another of the giant state companies that dominate the Chinese industry.
在負責國際交易的中國政府企業中海油擔任公司領導期間，傅成玉收購優尼科慘敗，此事看上去斷送了他的前途。但傅成玉挺了過來，並於去年開始掌管另一個主導中國工業的國有行業巨頭中石化集團。
He said diplomatically that Sinopec, a much larger company, is “more traditional in terms of management” than Cnooc, whose mandate is to develop offshore oil and gas. One suspects that Mr. Fu is using diplomatic language for bureaucratic.
他婉轉地表示，中石化這樣一家大企業“在管理上比中海油更加傳統”，中海油是負責開發海外油氣資源的公司。也許，他是在使用外交辭令，所謂的“傳統”，其實是“官僚”的意思。
He said that the upside of Sinopec’s needing a lot of work meant “there is a great deal of potential to add value to the company.”
他說，中石化需要做大量的工作，從好的方面來說，這意味着“有很大的潛力，可以為公司創造更大的價值”。
He said he was pleased that the company had outperformed the market recently.
他說，近來公司的表現好於股市平均水平，他感到滿意。
Mr. Fu, who studied petroleum engineering at the University of Southern California, is intrigued by the shale gas and oil revolutions changing the U.S. energy picture.
在美國南加州大學(University of Southern California)學習石油工程專業的傅成玉，對改變美國能源前景的頁岩氣和石油革命很感興趣。
China, too, has plenty of potential for a shale gas boom, he said. He brushed off the notion that Chinese companies lacked the necessary technology to develop shale gas. Sinopec, for instance has already drilled many shale gas wells.
他說，中國也有大規模開採頁岩氣的潛力。他對中國企業缺乏必要的頁岩氣開採技術的說法不屑一顧。中石化就已經鑽探了很多頁岩氣井。
The companies can always acquire technology from service companies like Schlumberger and Halliburton, “as long as you have money,” he said.
他表示，“只要有錢”，中國企業總是可以從服務性公司，比如斯倫貝謝(Schlumberger)和哈里伯頓 (Halliburton)那裡購買技術。
He conceded that the Chinese industry would require time to catch up with the United States. The next five years will be “ a studying phase,” he said.
他承認，中國的工業水平要趕上美國，還需要時間。他說，接下來的五年將是“一個學習的階段”。
