数据显示10月中国经济增长加速

Encouraging Signs in Chinese Manufacturing Data

BETTINA WASSENER
2012年11月2日
HONG KONG — Two surveys, released Thursday, that take the pulse of the Chinese manufacturing sector buttressed the view that the giant Chinese economy was picking up a little speed after months of deceleration.
香港——本周四，两份把脉中国制造业的调查支持了下述观点，这就是，庞大的中国经济在经历了数月减速之后，开始略有回升。
The monthly polls — one conducted by the China Federation of Logistics and Purchasing in Beijing, the other released by the British bank HSBC — both showed that manufacturing conditions had improved in October as the effects of recent stimulus measures and somewhat improved overseas demand had begun to filter through to companies making products as varied as furniture, garments and plastic goods.
分别由北京的中国物流与采购联合会（简称CFLP）和英国汇丰银行(HSBC)发布的两份月度调查均显示，受近来财政刺激措施的影响，以及由于海外需求略有增长给家具、服装和塑料产品等制造厂家带来更多的订货，中国制造业的情况在10月有所改善。
The official purchasing managers index for October, from the logistics federation, came in at 50.2, rallying above the 50-point mark, which separates contraction from expansion, for the first time since July.
CFLP公布的官方数据显示，10月的中国采购经理人指数达到了50.2，是自今年7月以来第一次高于50这一收缩与扩张的分界线。
The HSBC reading, which is more focused on smaller and medium-size companies, rose to an eight-month high of 49.5 in October, up from 47.9 in September. The reading was also a significant improvement from the preliminary figure of 49.1 that the bank published last week.
汇丰银行公布的采购经理人指数更侧重于中小企业，中国的指数从9月的47.9上升到10月的49.5，是过去8个月里最高的。这一指数也比其上周公布的49.1的初步数据有较大的提高。
Over the past month or so, anecdotal evidence and early data have signaled that the Chinese economy might finally have bottomed out. Flagging overseas demand and the delayed effects of measures aimed at cooling inflation and a red-hot property market had caused China to lose steam this year.
在过去大约一个月里，传闻的证据和早期的数据已经预示，中国经济可能会最终触底。海外需求疲软，加上旨在冷却通胀和炙热的房地产市场所采取的措施的滞后效果，曾让今年的中国经济失去动力。
The indexes released Thursday, however, backed up the impression that the economy might enjoy a modest recovery in coming months.
然而，周四公布的指数加强了人们对中国经济可能在未来数月适度复苏的印象。
“Overall, this reflects the impact of policy easing that has accelerated since May, including pick-up in infrastructure investment, fiscal expenditure, accommodative liquidity condition and monetary policy stance,” economists at J.P. Morgan said in a note.
摩根大通(J.P. Morgan)的经济学家在一份简报中写道，“总的来说，这反映了自今年5月以来，加速的宽松政策所带来的影响，包括增加基础设施投资和财政支出，改善促使货币流动的条件和货币政策立场。”
A stabilization in the housing market in recent months also provided support to domestic demand, they added.
他们还写道，中国房地产市场在最近数月趋稳也为国内需求提供了支持。
The picture of modest improvements extended across much of the rest of Asia in October, HSBC purchasing managers indexes for other countries showed.
汇丰银行提供的其他国家采购经理人指数显示，亚洲其余部分许多国家的经济在10月份也都出现了适度回升。
The readings for Indonesia, South Korea and Taiwan all jumped more than one point from September.
印度尼西亚、韩国和台湾的采购经理人指数都比9月的增长了一个点。
South Korea also reported trade data Thursday that showed exports picking up again in October, with a 1.2 percent gain from a year earlier. Although the increase was small, it topped expectations and was a turnaround from the decline of 2 percent recorded in September.
同样于本周四发布的韩国贸易数据显示，韩国出口额10月再度出现回升，比去年同期上涨了1.2%。虽然这一增长额度较小，却超过预期，而且和9月出口额下滑2%相比，是一个好转。
Korean exports serve as a good bellwether for regional growth, said Ronald Man, an economist at HSBC in Hong Kong, and the data underlined expectations that growth would stabilize during the current quarter.
香港的汇丰银行经济学家罗纳德·曼(Ronald Man)表示，韩国的出口数据为本地区经济增长充当了一个好的风向标，这一数据增强了人们对区域经济会在当前季度趋稳的预期。
That said, Mr. Man wrote, “we remain cautious on the strength of the upward momentum.”
曼写道，虽然这样说，但“我们对经济上升的势头依然保持谨慎。”
Underlining that the upturn does not extend evenly across the region, moreover, the HSBC purchasing managers index for Vietnam slipped in October, to 48.7 points from 49.2 in September. The reading for India managed only the smallest of gains, edging up to 52.9 in October from 52.8 the previous month. And the figures for South Korea and Taiwan, although much improved, remained below 50.
而且，数据显示，经济好转的情况并不均衡遍及整个区域。根据汇丰银行的数据，越南的采购经理人指数从9月的49.2下滑到了10月的48.7。印度数据的上升微乎其微，从9月的52.8攀升至10月的52.9。韩国和台湾的数据虽然有所提升，但依然在50以下。
Economists also remained guarded about the prospects for the Chinese economy, which faces structural challenges and an aging population. Growth, analysts say, is unlikely to bounce back to the levels reached before the global financial crisis. The World Bank and the International Monetary Fund, for example, forecast that expansion next year would be a little more than 8 percent — up from a bit less than 8 percent this year.
经济学家也对中国的经济前景保持谨慎的态度，中国经济面临着结构性挑战和老龄化人口问题。经济学家认为，中国的经济增长不大可能恢复到全球经济危机之前的水平。例如，世界银行和国际货币基金组织预测，明年，中国的经济增速将略高于8%，比今年的低于8%要有所提升。
Moreover, financial turmoil in Europe and the government spending cuts and tax increases set to take effect in January in the United States could set back global growth again.
此外，欧洲金融危机和美国政府将于明年1月实施的增税减支措施可能会使全球经济再度受挫。
The “fragile external environment,” the J.P. Morgan team wrote, “remains the most notable risk in the road of economic recovery.”
摩根大通的经济学家写道，“脆弱的外部环境依然是经济复苏之路上最显要的风险。”
In the longer term, the leaders preparing to take the helm at the Chinese Communist Party congress that starts next week will have to introduce profound changes to the economy in order to safeguard sustained growth, many analysts say.
许多分析人士表示，从长期来说，准备在下周召开的中共党代会上接手政权的领导人必须推行深刻的经济改革才能保障经济持续增长。
Productivity in the domestic corporate sector — which is dominated by often inefficient state-owned companies — will have to rise. At the same time, the economy needs to be weaned from its current reliance on investment and exports and steered toward more consumption-driven growth, analysts at the ratings agency Standard & Poor’s said at a media briefing in Hong Kong on Thursday.
本周四，评级机构标准普尔公司(Standard & Poor’s)的分析师在香港举行的新闻发布会上表示，国内企业部门的生产力亟待提高，目前这个部门由通常效率较低的国有企业控制着。同时，中国经济需要摆脱现有的对投资和出口的依赖，更多地转向由消费驱动的增长模式。

翻译：张薇
