WTO裁定美国部分对华关税违反国际贸易规则

W.T.O. Says American Tariffs on China Broke Global Trade Rules

ANA SWANSON
2020年9月16日
WASHINGTON — A World Trade Organization panel said Tuesday that the United States violated international trade rules by imposing tariffs on China in 2018 in the middle of President Trump’s trade war.
华盛顿——世界贸易组织的一个委员会周二表示，美国在2018年特朗普总统的贸易战中对中国加征关税，违反了国际贸易规则。
The panel of trade experts sided with a complaint that China had filed, which argued that Mr. Trump’s tariffs violated several global rules, including a provision that requires all W.T.O. members to offer equal tariff rates among the body’s trading partners.
该贸易专家委员会支持中国称特朗普关税政策违反多项全球规则的申诉，包括一项要求所有世贸组织成员国在其贸易伙伴之间提供平等关税税率的条款。
Mr. Trump broke with that tradition. During his trade war with China, the president imposed tariffs on more than $360 billion worth of Chinese products, in an effort to persuade the country to strengthen its intellectual property protections and make other changes to policies that Mr. Trump said put American workers at a disadvantage. The administration drew on an American legal provision — called Section 301 — to impose the tariffs, which allows the president to restrict foreign commerce that unfairly burdens the United States.
特朗普打破了这一传统。在与中国的贸易战中，这位总统对价值3600多亿美元的中国产品加征关税，目的是说服中国加强知识产权保护，并对特朗普所称的对美国劳工不利的政策做出其他改变。特朗普政府利用被称为“301条款”的一项美国法律条款来征收关税，它允许总统限制对美国造成不公平负担的外国贸易。
The effect of the ruling remains unclear. The United States and China signed a trade deal in January, but the bulk of the tariffs imposed by the Trump administration remain in place, covering more than half of China’s exports to the United States.
该裁决的影响目前尚不清楚。美中在1月签订了一项贸易协定，但特朗普政府施加的大部分关税仍然存在，覆盖了中国对美国出口的一半以上。
广告
In a statement, Robert E. Lighthizer, the United States Trade Representative, blasted the World Trade Organization for trying to prevent the United States from helping its own workers.
美国贸易代表罗伯特·E·莱特希泽(Robert E. Lighthizer)在声明中抨击世贸组织阻止美国帮助自己的劳工。
“This panel report confirms what the Trump administration has been saying for four years: The W.T.O. is completely inadequate to stop China’s harmful technology practices,” Mr. Lighthizer said. “Although the panel did not dispute the extensive evidence submitted by the United States of intellectual property theft by China, its decision shows that the W.T.O. provides no remedy for such misconduct.”
“该委员会的报告证实了特朗普政府四年来一直在说的事情：世贸组织完全不能阻止中国有害的技术使用，”莱特希泽说。“尽管该委员会没有对美国提交的中国窃取知识产权的大量证据提出异议，但它的决定表明，世贸组织没有为此类不当行为提供解决办法。”
“The United States must be allowed to defend itself against unfair trade practices, and the Trump administration will not let China use the W.T.O. to take advantage of American workers, businesses, farmers and ranchers,” he added.
“必须允许美国保护自己不受不公平贸易行为的影响，特朗普政府不会让中国利用世贸组织来占美国劳工、企业、农民和农场主的便宜，”他补充道。
The United States has 60 days to respond to the decision. But the ruling may have little practical effect, since the United States effectively crippled the W.T.O. panel responsible for handling appeals of trade disputes, by refusing to appoint new members to it.
美国有60天时间对这一决定做出回应。但这项裁决可能实效甚微，因为美国通过拒绝任命新成员，实际上削弱了世贸组织负责处理贸易争端申诉的委员会。
If the United States chooses to fight the organization’s latest decision, the case will end up in a kind of legal limbo, with no resolution in sight.
如果美国选择反对该组织的最新决定，这桩案件将陷入某种法律上的僵局，看不到解决的希望。
“Before the Trump administration came along and decimated the W.T.O. appellate body, a case like this would be appealed,” said Chad P. Bown, a senior fellow at the Peterson Institute for International Economics.
“在特朗普政府上台并摧毁了世贸组织的上诉机构之前，像这样的案件是会被提起上诉的，”彼得森国际经济研究所(Peterson Institute for International Economics)高级研究员查德·P·鲍恩(Chad P. Bown)说。
广告
If the panel had enough members to function and its members upheld the ruling, the World Trade Organization could authorize China to retaliate if the United States did not change its policies or if the United States and China could not agree to some other form of compensation.
如果该委员会有足够的成员来正常运作，并且其成员支持裁决，在美国不改变其政策，或美中不能就其他补偿形式达成一致的情况下，世贸组织可以授权中国进行报复。
“But since the Trump administration has refused to appoint new members to the appellate body, the U.S. could appeal this panel report ‘into the void,’” Mr. Bown said.
“但由于特朗普政府拒绝为上诉机构任命新成员，美国只能拿着这份委员会报告‘对着空气’上诉，”鲍恩说。
If the United States chooses not to appeal but also makes no changes in response to the ruling, China could ask for the World Trade Organization’s permission to recoup some of its losses by imposing tariffs on the United States.
如果美国选择不上诉，但在裁决之下也不做任何改变，中国可以请求世贸组织批准对美国加征关税，以弥补其部分损失。
But Mr. Bown said the retaliation issue “is largely moot anyway,” since China responded to America’s tariffs with its own levies beginning in July 2018. Since China did not have W.T.O. permission to retaliate against the United States, those tariffs, too, were arguably a violation of global trade rules.
但鲍恩表示，讨论报复“在很大程度上也是没有实际意义的”，因为中国从2018年7月开始自己加征关税，以作为对美国关税的回应。由于中国对美国进行的报复没有得到世贸组织许可，这些关税也可以说是违反了全球贸易规则。
The Trump administration has continued to play an active role in the World Trade Organization, but many of Mr. Trump’s biggest trade offensives over the past three years have bypassed the organization’s rules.
特朗普政府仍在世贸组织中积极发挥作用，但在过去三年里，特朗普发起的许多最大的贸易攻势都绕开了该组织的规则。
While other countries have criticized the United States for weakening the global trading system, the Trump administration argues that the World Trade Organization is largely ineffective and badly in need of overhaul, in part because of its failure to police China’s unfair trade practices.
尽管其他国家批评美国削弱了全球贸易体系，但特朗普政府辩称，世贸组织几乎没有实际作用，亟需彻底改革，部分原因是该组织未能对中国的不公平贸易行为进行监管。
