美聯儲把短期利率與失業率掛鈎

Fed to Hold Rates Down Until Jobless Rate Is Below 6.5%

BINYAMIN APPELBAUM
2012年12月13日
WASHINGTON — The Federal Reserve said Wednesday it planned to hold short-term interest rates near zero so long as the unemployment rate remains above 6.5 percent, reinforcing its commitment to improve labor market conditions.
華盛頓——美聯儲(Federal Reserve)周三加強了其改善勞動力市場狀況的承諾，表示只要失業率維持在6.5%以上，就會把短期利率保持在接近於零的水平。
The Fed also said that it would continue in the new year its monthly purchases of $85 billion in Treasury bonds and mortgage-backed securities, the second prong of its effort to accelerate economic growth by reducing borrowing costs.
美聯儲還表示，它明年將繼續每月購進價值850億美元（約合5314億元人民幣）的政府長期債券和抵押擔保證券。這是美聯儲為通過降低貸款成本加速經濟增長做出的第二部分努力。
But Fed officials still do not expect the unemployment rate to fall below the new target for at least three more years, according to forecasts also published Wednesday, and they chose not to expand the Fed’s stimulus campaign.
但是，同在周三發佈的預測數據顯示，美聯儲官員仍然認為，至少在未來三年，失業率不會降至這個新的目標值以下，並且他們選擇不擴大美聯儲的經濟刺激行動。
At a press conference following the announcement, Fed Chairman Ben S. Bernanke said the central bank was constrained in its ability to do more.
在上述公告之後的新聞發佈會上，美聯儲主席本·S·伯南克(Ben S. Bernanke)表示，央行能力有限，無法做到更多。
“If we could wave a magic wand and get unemployment down to 5 percent tomorrow, obviously we would do that,” Mr. Bernanke said. “But there are constraints in terms of the dynamics of the economy, in terms of the power of these tools and in terms that we do need to take into account other costs and risks that might be associated with a large expansion of our balance sheet.”
“如果能揮動魔杖，讓失業率明天就降到5%，我們顯然會這麼干，”伯南克說，“但我們也面臨制約，制約來自經濟局勢、這些工具的能力以及我們必須考慮的可能因大幅擴張資產負債表而來的其他一些成本和風險。”
The announcements reinforced a policy shift that began in September, when the Fed first said it would keep buying bonds until the job market improved, breaking with decades during which limiting inflation was its constant priority.
前述公告加強了始於9月的政策轉變。當時，美聯儲首次表示會持續購買債券，直到就業市場得到改善為止，結束了此前幾十年該機構一直以限制通脹為首要任務的傳統。
As in September, the Fed sought to make clear on Wednesday that it is not responding to evidence of new economic problems, but instead increasing its efforts to address existing problems that have restrained growth for more than three years. The most recent jobless rate, for November, was 7.7 percent.
和9月一樣，美聯儲在周三試圖讓人們明白，自己的行動不是在回應新的經濟問題出現的徵兆，而是在加大解決現有問題的努力，這些問題制約經濟增長的時間已經長達三年多。最新的失業率數據來自11月，為7.7%。
Mr. Bernanke said that the decision to publish economic objectives “makes more explicit the F.O.M.C.’s intention to maintain accommodation for as long as needed,” referring to the Fed’s open market committee.
伯南克稱，公布經濟目標的決定“更加清楚地表明了聯邦公開市場委員會（Federal Open Market Committee，簡稱FOMC）在必要的時間裡維持寬鬆政策的意圖。”
The economic forecasts published Wednesday show that Fed officials expect the economy to expand 2.3 percent to 3 percent in 2013, slightly below the September forecast of growth of 2.5 percent to 3 percent.  The economic projections showed most expect unemployment not to fall below the target of 6.5 percent before the end of 2015.
周三公布的經濟預測數據顯示，美聯儲官員預計，2013年的經濟增長率介於2.3%到3%之間，略低於2.5%到3%的9月預測增幅。經濟預測表明，大多數人都預計，失業率不會在2015年年底之前降到6.5%的目標值以下。
Fed officials have repeatedly overestimated the health of the economy and the pace of the recovery in recent years and the latest changes, while relatively small, continue a pattern of steady retreats from initial optimism.
近年來，美聯儲官員多次高估了經濟的健康程度和復蘇步伐，此次最新調整雖然相對較小，但卻繼續了預測由最初的樂觀估計逐步回落的趨勢。
The forecasts remain optimistic in at least one respect. They assume that Congress and the White House reach a deal to avert scheduled tax increases and spending cuts next year. If not, Fed officials agree that their own efforts will be trivial in comparison, and the economy likely will return to recession.
預測數據至少在有一點上依然很樂觀。他們預計，國會和白宮將達成協議，防止在明年實行計劃中的增稅和削減開支。美聯儲官員一致認為，如果雙方不能達成協議，他們自己的努力只能起到相對說來微不足道的作用，經濟也多半會再次陷入衰退。
