无锡尚德迫于中方银行压力破产

Chinese Solar Panel Maker Falters as Prices Plunge

KEITH BRADSHER
2013年3月21日
HONG KONG — Suntech Power, a Chinese manufacturer that became the world’s largest producer of solar panels by 2011 only to be battered by plummeting prices, announced on Wednesday evening that its main operating subsidiary had been pushed into bankruptcy by eight Chinese banks.
香港——周三晚上，中国制造商尚德太阳能电力有限公司(Suntech Power)宣布其主要生产基地在八家中国银行的压力下破产。该公司曾在2011年成为世界上最大的太阳能组件制造商，此后却因价格不断下跌遭受重创。
Suntech was the Icarus of the solar panel industry, with production that soared year after year on heavy investment, as Western investors bought up its New York-traded shares and its international debt issues. Part of a massive Chinese government effort to dominate renewable energy industries, Suntech grew to 10,000 employees in its hometown of Wuxi on China’s east coast and even set up a small factory in Arizona to do further assembly of panels there.
尚德是太阳能组件行业里的伊卡洛斯（Icarus，希腊神话人物，飞上天之后坠亡——译注），随着西方投资者将其在纽约交易的股份和国际债券抢购一空，该公司产量在大笔投资的支持下年年激增。作为中国政府主导可再生能源行业大举努力的一部分，位于中国东海岸城市无锡的尚德总部扩大规模，员工数量增达一万人，该公司甚至在亚利桑那州建起了一个小型工厂，进一步组装太阳能组件。
But a tenfold expansion of overall Chinese solar panel manufacturing capacity from 2008 to 2012 pushed down prices of solar panels about 75 percent, undermining the economics of the business. Rapid expansion of natural gas production in the United States and a curtailment of subsidies in the European Union also hurt solar panel prices, as did an American imposition last year of import tariffs totaling about 40 percent after an anti-dumping and anti-subsidy investigation.
但是，2008至2012年间，中国太阳能组件制造能力增长了10倍，致使太阳能组件价格降低了75%左右，损害了这一行业的效益。美国天然气产量的快速增长以及欧盟削减补助的措施也给太阳能组件的价格带来不利影响，此外，美国去年还在一项反倾销和反补贴调查之后对太阳能组件征收了40%的进口关税。
The European Union is also completing its own trade investigation of Chinese solar panel shipments that could lead to steep tariffs there as well.
欧盟也即将完成对进口中国太阳能组件的贸易调查，调查可能也会导致高额关税。
Ocean Yuan, the president of Grape Solar, an importer of solar panels that is based in Eugene, Oregon, said he foresaw a series of bankruptcies by big Chinese solar panel manufacturers, some of which have very high debt like Suntech. Chinese manufacturers lost as much as $1 for every $3 of sales last year as they struggled to keep factories open despite plummeting prices.
位于俄勒冈尤金的太阳能电池板进口商Grape Solar公司总裁袁海洋(Ocean Yuan)称，他预计会有一系列中国太阳能组件制造商陷入破产，其中一些公司和尚德一样债台高筑。去年，中国制造商不顾价格节节下跌，依然竭力维持工厂运转，结果是每3美元的销售都会带来高达1美元的损失。
“They are bleeding every day: the more they sell, the more they lose money,” Mr. Yuan wrote in an e-mail.
袁海洋在一封邮件中称，“他们每天都在流血：卖得越多，亏得越多。”
He predicted that European and U.S. solar panel manufacturers would also face crippling financial pressures and that the long-term survivors in the industry would be manufacturers in Taiwan, who have low costs and are not subject to the American import tariffs or the likely European tariffs.
他预计欧洲和美国的太阳能组件制造商也将面临严重的经济压力，长期看来，能在这一行业中存活下来的只有台湾制造商，因为他们的成本较低，而且不受美国进口关税和欧洲类似关税的约束。
The Chinese banks quietly asked a court on Monday in Wuxi to declare the operating subsidiary, Wuxi Suntech, insolvent and begin restructuring it. The operating subsidiary notified the court on Wednesday that it did not object to the insolvency petition.
周一，中国几家银行悄悄地向无锡一家法院提交了申请，要求宣布尚德的生产基地无锡尚德破产，并开始对该企业进行重组。周三，无锡尚德通知法院，公司对破产重组的要求没有异议。
Suntech Power, the parent, said that it was not filing for bankruptcy and would continue to honor warranties on the company’s solar panels.
其母公司尚德太阳能电力有限公司称，公司没有申请破产，并将继续为本公司生产的太阳能组件提供质量保障。
The bankruptcy filing is widely expected to lead to a takeover of the Wuxi operations by Wuxi Guolian Development Group, a financial conglomerate controlled by the city government of Wuxi.
人们广泛认为，该公司破产之后，将会被无锡市政府控股的金融集团无锡国联发展集团接管。
A woman answering the phones at Wuxi Guolian’s headquarters said on March 13 that her company was involved in a Suntech acquisition but declined to provide details or her name.
3月13日，无锡国联总部一位接听电话的女士称，该公司正在对一家尚德公司进行收购，但拒绝提供细节和自己的姓名。
On Tuesday, Suntech Power announced the appointment of a new president, Weiping Zhou, a longtime Wuxi Guolian executive who had previously been the chairman of the company’s futures trading subsidiary.
周二，尚德太阳能电力有限公司宣布任命长期担任无锡国联高管的周卫平为新总裁。此前，周卫平曾担任国联期货有限公司董事长。
In its statement announcing the bankruptcy of Wuxi Suntech, Suntech Power did not mention any immediate role for Wuxi Guolian. David King, the chief executive of Suntech Power, said that the company “will continue to work closely with all of our stakeholders and take the necessary steps to put Suntech back on track for growth.”
在宣布无锡尚德破产的声明中，尚德太阳能电力有限公司没有提及无锡国联与此事的任何直接联系。尚德太阳能电力有限公司首席执行官金纬(David King)称，该公司“将继续与所有股东紧密合作，并采取必要措施推动尚德重回增长轨道”。
Suntech Power is traded on the New York Stock Exchange, and had a market capitalization of $16 billion at its peak. The company failed to make payments last Friday on $541 million worth of convertible debt.
尚德太阳能电力有限公司已在纽约证券交易所(New York Stock Exchange)上市，高峰时市值曾达160亿美元。上周五，该公司未能支付价值5.41亿美元的可转债。
The fact that Suntech Power’s Chinese subsidiary filed for bankruptcy protection without the parent also filing may cause acrimony over whether foreign creditors are being treated unfairly compared with domestic creditors in China.
尚德太阳能电力有限公司的中国分公司申请了破产保护，总公司却没有申请，这一事实可能会引发人们不满，怀疑公司对外国债权人执行了与中国债权人不同的不公平政策。
It is rare for Chinese companies to file for bankruptcy, as the government has sometimes stepped in first to help them and avoid damaging the broader reputation of Chinese companies’ creditworthiness.
中国公司很少申请破产，因为政府有时会首先介入提供帮助，避免损害中国公司的整体信誉。
The insolvency filing by Wuxi Suntech sets off a potentially complex legal fight that could damage international investors’ faith in Chinese bonds, after many months of scandals that have already badly damaged investors’ enthusiasm for the overseas-listed shares of Chinese companies.
无锡尚德申请破产一事引发了一场可能会十分复杂的诉讼战，这可能会损害国际投资者对中国债券的信心。此前，连年累月的种种丑闻已经严重损害了投资者对在海外上市的中国公司的热情。
Wuxi Suntech holds virtually all of the factories and other physical assets of Suntech Power, and these assets are in China, which makes it easier for the Chinese banks to take ownership of them. Foreign lenders to Suntech Power, like the investors who bought its convertible bonds, are “structurally subordinate” to the Chinese lenders in trying to recover their money from these assets, according to legal experts.
一些法律专家称，无锡尚德实际上拥有尚德公司所有的工厂以及其他实物资产，而这些资产在中国，更容易被中国的银行接管。与中国债权人相比，尚德公司的国外债权人，比如那些购买其可转债的投资者，在试图从这些资产中拿回自己资金时处于“结构性劣势”。
But most of the Suntech group’s sales were in the United States and Europe, because China’s renewable energy policies tended to emphasize manufacturing subsidies that would allow Chinese companies to capitalize on foreign governments’ subsidies for consumers. Foreign lenders could have an advantage in seizing dollars and euros in overseas accounts, as well as the money that overseas buyers still owe Suntech, which allowed many creditors to delay paying for panels until months after they were delivered.
不过，尚德集团的大部分销售业务都在美国和欧洲，这是因为，中国的可再生能源政策往往侧重于补贴生产商，由此使中国企业可以利用外国政府给消费者提供的补贴。外国债权人在通过海外账户截留美元和欧元方面占有优势、在追索尚德的海外买家欠款方面也占优势。尚德允许许多货主延迟支付太阳能组件货款，直到出货后的几个月再行支付。
Another complexity is that Suntech Power is incorporated in the Cayman Islands. The bankruptcy laws there usually do not allow restructurings, with companies tending to be liquidated instead. But the convertible bonds issued by Suntech Power specified that any dispute involving the bonds would have to be resolved under New York State law.
另一个复杂之处在于，尚德太阳能电力有限公司的注册地是开曼群岛。那里的破产法通常不允许公司重组，那里的公司往往会转而进行清算。不过，尚德太阳能电力有限公司发行的可转债已经列明，任何涉及可转债的纠纷都必须按照纽约州的法律解决。
Last week, Suntech closed its factory in Goodyear, Arizona. It was an operation of just 43 people who put aluminum frames and electrical junction boxes on solar cells from China so that they could qualify for “Buy American” programs.
上周，尚德关闭了在亚利桑那州古德伊尔的工厂。这是一家仅有43名员工的工厂，工人们把铝框和接线盒安装在产自中国的太阳能电池上，使其符合“购买美国货”(Buy American)条款的要求。
In announcing the bankruptcy of Wuxi Suntech, the corporate parent did not specify the debts of its subsidiary. Xinhua, the Chinese state-run news agency, said Wuxi Suntech owed 7.1 billion renminbi, or $1.1 billion, to nine Chinese banks; Xinhua did not explain why only eight banks joined the insolvency petition, or the extent of the loans to Wuxi Suntech by the unidentified ninth bank.
在宣布无锡尚德破产时，其母公司没有具体言明无锡尚德的负债情况。据中国官方通讯社新华社报道，无锡尚德对九家中国银行欠下了总额为71亿元人民币的债务；新华社没有解释为何只有八家银行提出清偿请求，也没有说明身份不明的第九家银行的贷款规模。
The bankruptcy of Wuxi Suntech comes after a dozen solar panel manufacturers in the United States and a dozen in Europe have either failed or cut back production considerably after finding that they were unable to cover their costs at current low prices for solar panels.
无锡尚德破产之前，美国和欧洲各有十几家太阳能组件生产商宣布破产或大幅削减产量，因为他们发现，以太阳能组件现在的低售价，他们无法收回成本。
The industry’s problem is that most of the cost of a solar panel lies in building the factory, not in operating the equipment. So when the industry has severe overcapacity, as it does now, each company continues running its factories so as to cover its tiny operating costs plus at least a small part of the interest on the loans it took out to buy the costly factory equipment.
该行业的问题在于，太阳能组件的大部分成本产生于厂房建设，而不是设备运行。因此，当整个行业像目前这样出现严重产能过剩的时候，每个公司都让工厂继续运转，以抵消微不足道的运营成本，再抵偿至少一小部分的贷款利息。公司当初贷款则是为了购买价格高昂的工厂设备。
But when every company pursues that strategy, the whole industry loses money and virtually no business is able to cover its full interest costs.
不过，当每个公司都贯彻这种策略时，整个行业就出现了亏损，实质上，没有哪家企业能够收回所有的利息成本。
