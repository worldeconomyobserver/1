中海油對百億美元大收購「充滿信心」

China's Bid for Nexen May Survive a Rejection

IAN AUSTEN
2012年11月21日
OTTAWA - The rejection of a major energy industry takeover by Canadian government may not indicate that it is adopting a harder line on mergers involving foreign companies.
渥太華——加拿大政府拒絕一家大型能源公司的收購計劃可能並不意味着加拿大對外國公司正在採取強硬路線。
Last month, Canada used foreign investment laws to reject a $5.17 billion takeover of Progress Energy Resources by Petronas, the Malaysian state-owned energy company.
上月，加拿大根據外國投資法拒絕了馬來西亞國家石油公司(Petronas)以51.7億美元（約合322億元人民幣）收購進步能源公司(Progress Energy Resources)的計劃。
That decision led to speculation on the outcome of a $15 billion bid for Nexen, another Canadian energy company, by China National Offshore Oil Corporation, the Chinese state-run oil producer known as Cnooc (pronounced SEE-nook).
這個決定引發人們對中國國有石油公司中國海洋石油總公司(China National Offshore Oil Corporation)以150億美元競購另一家加拿大能源公司尼克森公司(Nexen)結果的猜測。
[Canada has been conducting a review to determine whether a takeover by the Chinese state-owned enterprise would bring a "net benefit" to Canada. Ottawa said on November 2 that it has extended the review by a month to December 10.
加拿大正在評估中國國有企業收購加拿大公司是否會給加拿大帶來“凈利潤”。11月2日，渥太華官方稱，加拿大已將評估延長了一個月，到12月10日止。
[The extension was not a surprise, CNOOC Chairman Wang Yilin said this month, Reuters reported. "It is a big acquisition worth $15.1 billion. It would be our biggest-ever overseas acquisition, and because of the sheer size of the project, the government and relevant authorities... will need to handle large amounts of review work," he said. 
這次延期並不出人意料，路透社消息報道，本月中海油董事長王宜林稱，“這筆價格151億美元的收購規模很大。這筆交易如果成功，將是中國企業最大的一單海外收購。由於項目龐大，政府有關部門、公司所在國家必然要進行具體的審查工作。”
["We are fully confident that the deal will succeed," Wang told reporters on the sidelines of the Communist Party congress in Beijing. "We expect the transaction to be completed by the year-end."]
“我們充滿信心，這筆交易能成功，”王宜林對採訪中共十八大的記者說，“我們希望這筆交易能在年底完成。”
The Progress deal was the third foreign takeover turned down by the current Conservative government using a law that previously was mainly known for rubber-stamping acquisitions. All three rejections - the others involved a potash mining company and an aerospace company - were unexpected given the antiregulation bent of the government and Stephen Harper, the prime minister.
進步能源公司的交易是當前保守派政府利用法律規定拒絕的第三起收購案。以前，該項法律主要是用於批准收購項目。所有三起案例——另外兩起包括一家鉀資源開採公司和一家航空公司——都出乎人們意料，因為加拿大政府和總理斯蒂芬·哈珀(Stephen Harper)本人都有去行政監管的傾向。
The review system was introduced by a Conservative government in 1985 to replace a stronger law introduced in a wave of nationalism in the 1970s. Instead of requiring specific commitments in areas like jobs, technology and Canadian management, the law requires only that a takeover provide a "net benefit" for Canada.
這項評審系統是保守派政府在1985年建立的，取代了20世紀70年代民族主義浪潮中開始的一項更為嚴格的法律。這項法律只要求收購能給加拿大帶來“凈利潤”，而並不對就業、科技和加拿大管理方面提出具體要求。
But net benefit is not clearly defined in the law. Instead, it is defined on a case-by-case basis, with the prime minister as the final judge. Calls from investors and businesses recently led the Conservatives to start developing clearer guidelines, which it will make public. That process may also have led to the rejection of the Progress deal.
然而法律並沒有清楚定義什麼是凈利潤。只是具體案例具體分析，由總理做最終決策。最近，來自投資人和企業的呼聲導致保守派開始制訂更明確的指導原則，這些指導原則將會公之於眾。這個過程可能也是導致進步能源公司交易被拒的原因。
Although Canada's foreign investment review process is secretive and political, it appears that impatience by Petronas, rather than the merits of its bid, led to the rejection. And that rejection is not the last word in this case.
雖然加拿大的外國投資評審過程是保密的而且有政治色彩，但引起被拒的原因似乎在於馬來西亞國家石油公司耐心不足，而非競標的優劣。而且這次拒絕也不是該案的最終結果。
The Globe and Mail and The National Post, citing unnamed sources, reported said that the government asked Petronas last month if it could delay its decision until December so that it would follow the release of its new, broad foreign takeover guidelines.
《環球郵報》(The Globe and Mail)和《全國郵報》(The National Post)引用沒有署名的消息來源稱，加拿大政府上月詢問馬拉西亞國家石油公司是否可以推遲到12月再做決定，這樣就可以參照加拿大最新發佈的外國投資指導原則。
Petronas, the reports said, was already frustrated by the process and any further delay would complicate deadlines under its agreement with Progress. So it turned down the government's extension request. The rejection followed.
該報道稱，馬來西亞國有石油公司已經為審批過程感到懊惱，任何進一步的推延都會使其與進步能源公司協議的最終日期複雜化。所以它拒絕了加拿大政府的延期要求。接着加拿大就拒絕了該交易。
After the deal was rejected, however, the government indicated that the process was not over, and Petronas and Progress said in a statement that they would continue to work with the government to try to get their deal approved.
然而，這項交易被拒絕之後，加拿大政府表示，這一過程還未結束。馬拉西亞國有石油公司和進步能源公司在一項聲明中稱，他們將繼續與政府交流來促使這項交易獲得批准。
Politics aside, the Nexen deal with Cnooc faces different hurdles than the Petronas offer.
除了政治因素外，尼克森與中海油之間面臨的障礙，與馬拉西亞國有石油公司面臨的不同。
Although Nexen's assets are mainly outside Canada, it is a significant investor in Alberta's oil sands. It owns 65 percent of one sands project, with Cnooc as its minority partner, and has stakes in two other oil sands projects.
雖然尼克森的資產主要是在加拿大以外，但它卻是亞伯達油砂的一個重要投資者。尼克森擁有一個油砂項目的65%，還持有其他兩個油砂項目的股份，中海油是它的一個主要合作夥伴。
Some have expressed concern about a Chinese state-owned company gaining control of Nexen's Canadian assets, which include natural gas and shale gas properties.
一些人擔心中國國有企業獲得對尼克森加拿大資產的控制權，其中包括天然氣和頁岩氣的所有權。
Although Mr. Harper, the prime minister, does not usually tolerate criticism from within his party, some Conservative members of Parliament are among opponents of the Nexen deal. Rob Anders, a Conservative legislator who wants conditions placed on Cnooc, described China last month as being "a nonbenevolent actor and a strategic adversary."
雖然通常總理哈珀並不容忍自己黨內的反對聲音，但一些國會保守派成員卻站在了反對尼克森交易的一邊。保守派立法者羅布·安德斯(Rob Anders)希望給中海油一些限制條件，他在上個月稱，中國“來者不善，是戰略上的對手。”
But under Mr. Harper, and some predecessors, individual members of Parliament, even from his party, hold little sway.
但是在哈珀，以及幾位前任總理執政時期，個別國會議員，即使是來自總理的黨派的，都沒有多大影響力。
Since American politics stalled plans to deliver more oil sands production to the United States through the Keystone XL pipeline, Mr. Harper has focused on China as Canada's next great energy market and courted Chinese fuel investment. That, analysts say, is likely to guide his thinking on Cnooc and Nexen more than what happens with Petronas.
自從美國因政治因素阻斷了經由Keystone XL輸油管道向美國運輸更多油砂產品以後，哈珀已經將中國作為下一個巨大的能源市場，追求中國在燃料產業上的投資。分析人士稱，與馬來西亞國有石油公司的交易相比，這可能會更多地影響他對中海油和尼克森之間交易的想法。
