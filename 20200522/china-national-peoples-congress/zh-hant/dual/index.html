中國召開人大會議，在危機中展示力量

China, Faced With Challenges, Mounts Show of Strength at Party Congress

KEITH BRADSHER, 儲百亮
2020年5月22日
Sign up for NYT Chinese-language Morning Briefing.
（歡迎點擊此處訂閱NYT簡報，我們將在每個工作日發送最新內容至您的郵箱。）
BEIJING — China’s top leaders on Friday made a show of strength to confront defiance in Hong Kong and the economic damage wrought by the coronavirus outbreak, even as they acknowledged that both had dealt a blow to the ruling Communist Party’s agenda.
北京——面對香港的公然反抗和冠狀病毒疫情帶來的經濟損失，中國最高領導層在週五做出了力量的展示，儘管他們承認，這兩件事都對中共的執政議程造成了打擊。
On Hong Kong, the leadership struck a hard line at the annual meeting of China’s legislature, unveiling a plan to impose sweeping new security laws that would place the territory more firmly under Beijing’s thumb and crack down on antigovernment protests. But the move is likely to incite more unrest and outrage in the semiautonomous territory as well as criticism from abroad.
針對香港問題，領導層在中國立法機關的年度會議上公布了一項全面實施新安全法的計劃，意味著北京將對香港進行更嚴厲的掌控，並打擊反政府抗議活動。但此舉可能會在這一半自治地區引發更多動盪和憤怒，並招致更多海外批評。
On the economy, the premier, addressing the opening of the National People’s Congress, declared that the government had achieved a “decisive victory” against the coronavirus outbreak and that the country has shown great resilience. But in a break with tradition, China abandoned setting an annual growth target for 2020, recognizing the difficulties in restarting its economy amid a pandemic.
針對經濟問題，國務院總理在全國人民代表大會的開幕致辭上宣稱，政府在抗擊冠狀病毒疫情中取得了「決定性成果」，全國都展現了堅韌不拔的精神。但與以往傳統不同的是，中國放棄了預定的2020年增長目標，承認在大流行期間重啟經濟存在困難。
廣告
By embracing the challenges ahead, China’s leaders provided a call to the world that the party was emerging confidently from both crises with a greater resolve to defend its authority.
為了迎接未來的挑戰，中國領導層向世界發出了一個信號，那就是黨將會以更大的決心捍衛自身權威，自信地走出這兩場危機。
The congress is normally a symbolic annual gathering of the country’s political elite. This year, the symbolism matters more than usual. Xi Jinping, China’s top leader, has sought to project strength as the government tries to revive the economy, restart schools and businesses and claim credit for largely ending the epidemic that spread from Wuhan in central China.
 全國人民代表大會通常是中國政治精英的象徵性年度聚會。今年，這種象徵意義比以往更加重要。隨著政府試圖重振經濟、重啟學校和企業，並明確自己在這場始於中部城市武漢、目前基本得到控制的疫情中發揮的作用，中國最高領導人習近平試圖展示自己的力量。
Premier Li Keqiang, who is second-ranked in the Communist Party hierarchy behind Mr. Xi, made his speech to nearly 3,000 congress delegates who wore masks as they sat in neat rows in the ornate Great Hall of the People. He pledged to help blunt the impact of the slowdown with goals to limit inflation and unemployment.
作為在習近平之後的黨內二號人物，李克強總理在華麗的人民大會堂向近3000名戴著口罩的人大代表發表了講話。他承諾以限制通貨膨脹和失業率為目標，幫助減輕經濟下行的影響。
“At present and for some time to come, China will face challenges like never before,” he said. “However, we have unique political and institutional strengths, a strong economic foundation, enormous market potential and hundreds of millions of intelligent and hardworking people.”
「當前和今後一個時期，我國發展面臨風險挑戰前所未有，」他說。「但我們有獨特政治和制度優勢、雄厚經濟基礎、巨大市場潛力，億萬人民勤勞智慧。」
“The horizons for China’s development are full of promise,” Mr. Li said.
「中國的發展必將充滿希望，」李克強說。
The congress also outlined the party’s plan, disclosed in a surprise move on Thursday night, for new laws in Hong Kong to prevent and punish secession, subversion and foreign infiltration that it has blamed for fueling unrest in the city. The legislation would also allow the mainland’s feared security agencies to set up their operations publicly in Hong Kong for the first time, instead of operating on a limited scale in secrecy.
這次大會還概述了黨的一項計劃，這是在週四晚間出人意料被公布的內容，即在香港製定新法律，以防範和懲處被中共指責造成動盪的分裂顛覆活動和外部勢力滲透。這項法律還將允許大陸令人畏懼的安全機關首次在香港公開設立機構，而不是在有限範圍內祕密展開活動。
廣告
In a speech detailing the plan, Wang Chen, a Politburo member and first vice chairman of the congress, pointed to the protesters in Hong Kong who defaced the national flag and surrounded Beijing’s offices in the city as posing a threat to China’s sovereignty. He also cited long-held suspicions by Beijing that foreign governments had incited the recent protests in Hong Kong, even though evidence to support this is limited.
在詳細介紹該草案的講話中，全國人大常委會副委員長王晨指出，香港抗議者污損國旗，圍攻北京駐港機構，嚴重危害了國家主權。他還提到了北京長期以來的懷疑，即外國政府煽動了香港最近的抗議活動，儘管支持這一觀點的證據有限。
“Law-based and forceful measures must be taken to prevent, stop and punish such activities,” Mr. Wang declared, as delegates in the hall burst into applause.
「這些行為和活動，必須採取有力措施依法予以防範、制止和懲治，」王晨宣稱，大廳裡的代表們都爆發出熱烈的掌聲。
Beijing’s security plans drew immediate alarm, including in the Hong Kong stock market, which slumped more than 5 percent on Friday.
北京的安全法草案立即引發了恐慌，香港股市週五跌幅超過了5%。
The annual congress, which usually convenes in early March for about two weeks, had been delayed and shortened to a week this year because of the coronavirus crisis.
人大會議通常在3月初召開，為期約兩週，但由於冠狀病毒危機，今年的大會被推遲並縮短至一週。
By this week, the coronavirus outbreak had infected more than 89,000 people in China, including over 4,600 who died from the virus. Delegates opened the session with exactly 60 seconds of silence for the victims of the outbreak.
 截至本週，冠狀病毒疫情在中國已經感染了超過89000人，死亡4600多人。大會召開前，代表們以60秒的默哀表達了對疫情死難者的哀悼。
The unusual arrangements for the congress meeting this year reflect continued worries that China has not totally contained the outbreak. Most journalists have practically no access to the Great Hall, where the congress meets, and must instead follow proceedings and join news conferences over video links.Delegates have been required to undergo nucleic acid tests for the virus before they are even allowed to travel to Beijing. Hong Kong delegates were required to travel across the border to Shenzhen in mainland China so as to follow a standardized testing procedure.
今年全國人大會議的不尋常安排反映了人們對中國尚未完全控制疫情的持續擔憂。大多數記者實際上無法進入大會堂，必須通過影片鏈接跟蹤會議進程並參加新聞發布會。代表們被要求甚至在前往北京之前就進行病毒核酸檢測。香港代表被要求跨境前往中國大陸的深圳，以便遵循標準化的檢測程序。
On the agenda are proposals to improve the country’s disease surveillance system and hospitals, after the coronavirus outbreak exposed systematic failings. Investors are also looking to the meeting — which will approve a long-delayed budget for 2020 — to offer a clear sense of direction in the economy.
在新冠病毒暴露出系統性缺陷後，會議議程上也有改進國家疾病監測系統和醫院的建議。投資者還希望此次會議能夠為經濟提供一個明確的方向。此次會議將批准一份拖延已久的2020年預算。
Mr. Li’s budget proposal calls for an economic stimulus program equal to about 2 percent of the country’s economic output last year. 
李克強的預算提案要求採取一個相當於去年中國經濟產出2%的經濟刺激計劃。
China’s top financial planners have been leery of having the government borrow yet more money now to hand out checks to the public the way governments in the United States, Hong Kong and elsewhere have done. Debt-fueled stimulus programs helped the Chinese economy rebound quickly from the global financial crisis a decade ago but left the country burdened with debt and awash in wasteful projects.
美國、香港和其他地方的政府通過借更多錢向公眾發放支票，而中國高層的金融規劃者對於這樣的措施一直持謹慎態度。十年前，以債務為動力的刺激計劃幫助中國經濟從全球金融危機中迅速反彈，但也讓中國背負沉重的債務，並且增添了大量浪費的工程。
廣告
Mr. Li announced instead a series of small measures that are likely to be popular but will have a modest cost. He said that the government would cut the cost of broadband Internet access this year by 15 percent, for example. And he said that the government would increase its subsidies for basic medical insurance for rural residents and urban non-working residents — but only by a little over $4 a year per person.
李克強宣布的一系列小規模措施反而可能會受歡迎，但只需要付出適度的代價。例如，他說政府將在今年將寬帶互聯網接入成本降低15%。他還說，政府將增加對農村居民和城市無業居民的基本醫療保險補貼，但每人每年僅增加30元。
Mr. Li emphasized that despite the economic slowdown, there would be no retreat from eradicating persistent poverty by the end of this year, a goal that Mr. Xi has made a pillar of his man-of-the-people image.
儘管經濟放緩，但李克強強調，到今年年底消除貧困的目標不會變。習近平把該目標作為自己人民領袖形象的重要支撐。
Military spending will also continue to grow, with budget documents released by Mr. Li saying that it would increase by 6.6 percent this year even as overall central government spending is slated to fall 0.2 percent.
軍費開支也將繼續增長，李克強公布的預算草案顯示，儘管今年中央政府總體開支預計將下降0.2%，但軍費開支仍將增長6.6%。
Such an increase in military spending is likely to represent an expansion in its share of the entire country’s output, coming as economists are predicting growth of only around 1 to 2 percent this year.
這樣的一種增長意味著，軍費開支在國家總產出中所佔比例會升高，據經濟學家預測，今年的經濟增長率只有1%～2%左右。
Mr. Li made a clear effort to reassure domestic and foreign investors that China remains committed to its gradual evolution away from central planning toward a greater reliance on markets.
李克強向國內外的投資者明確保證，中國將繼續致力於逐步從中央計劃轉向更多依靠市場。
“We need to pursue reform and opening up as a means to stabilize employment, ensure people’s well-being, stimulate consumption, energize the market, and achieve stable growth,” he said. He did not address recent trends that have pointed to the contrary, such as the sharp shift by the state-controlled banking system toward more loans to state-owned enterprises instead of private enterprises.
「要用改革開放辦法，穩就業、保民生、促消費，拉動市場、穩定增長，」他說。他並沒有提到最近與此相反的趨勢，比如國有銀行突然增加對國有企業的放貸，民營企業融資更為困難。
He reaffirmed China’s commitment to the phase 1 trade agreement with the United States, which has allowed the United States to retain a wide range of tariffs on Chinese goods while China resumes buying a lot of American pork and other farm products. Because of the pandemic, China has fallen far behind that agreement’s targets for imports of American manufactured goods and energy.
他重申要落實中美第一階段經貿協議，該協議允許美國對中國商品保留廣泛的關稅，而中國將恢復購買美國豬肉和其他農產品。由於疫情的蔓延，中國已經遠遠落後於該協議規定的購買美國製成品和能源產品的目標。
廣告
The Chinese premier’s annual work report generally steers away from commenting directly on diplomatic feuds, and Mr. Li did not directly speak to tensions with the Trump administration, which has accused Beijing of delays and cover-ups that allowed the virus outbreak expand into a pandemic.
中國總理的年度政府工作報告通常不會就外交爭端直接表態，李克強也沒有直接提到與川普政府的緊張關係。川普政府指責北京的拖延和掩蓋使這次疫情發展成了大流行。
China has maintained that it acted quickly and transparently and Mr. Li signaled that China would continue to promote itself as a responsible partner in the global health crisis.
中國一直堅稱，它的行動迅速而透明，李克強的工作報告表明，中國將繼續在全球衛生危機中把自己宣傳為一個負責任的合作夥伴。
But at the same time, he also included a rare acknowledgement that China had made mistakes in handling the pandemic.
但與此同時，他也罕見地承認，中國在應對這次疫情方面犯了錯誤。
“Many weak links have been exposed in public health emergency management, and the people have expressed their views and suggestions, which deserve our attention,” he said.
他說：「公共衛生應急管理等方面暴露出不少薄弱環節，群眾還有一些意見和建議應予重視。」
