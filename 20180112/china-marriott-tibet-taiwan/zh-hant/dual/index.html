列西藏、台灣為「國家」，萬豪致歉：不支持分裂

Marriott to China: We Do Not Support Separatists

黃瑞黎
2018年1月12日
Marriott International, the hotel chain, wants to be clear on this point: It does not support separatist elements in China.
經營連鎖酒店的萬豪國際集團(Marriott International)想澄清一件事：那就是，它不支持中國的分裂分子。
Marriott is best known for its hospitality, not fomenting rebellion in the countries where it does business. Nevertheless, it apologized to the Chinese government on Thursday for the way it worded a customer survey — wording that has spurred intense online criticism and a government investigation.
讓萬豪酒店出名的是殷勤待客，而不是在公司做生意的國家裡煽動叛亂。儘管如此，這家公司週四向中國政府道歉，原因是它在一份客戶調查問卷中用詞不當，這份問卷已在網上引發了激烈的批評，還引起了政府的調查。
The problem: The survey listed Tibet, a region of China, and Taiwan, an island it claims as its own, as separate countries, according to Chinese media reports. It also listed Hong Kong and Macau — territories that are ruled by China — as separate, according to the reports.
問題出在這裡：據中國媒體報導，問卷把中國地區西藏以及中國堅持認為屬於自己的台灣列為獨立的國家。據報導，問卷還把中國統治的香港和澳門單獨列出來。
The error is a common one, and easy to make, among companies that do business there. A simple website drop-down menu labeled “countries” can inadvertently bestow the nation label on places like Taiwan and Hong Kong.
在中國做生意的企業中，這種錯誤很常見，而且很容易發生。網站上一個標著「國家」的簡單下拉菜單，無意中就給台灣和香港等地貼上了國家的標籤。
廣告
But in China, where the government is often quick to claim that the feelings of nearly 1.4 billion Chinese people have been hurt, the repercussions can be severe. The Shanghai Internet Information Office said it would shut down Marriott’s Chinese website and its Chinese app for a week starting today.
但在中國，這種錯誤的後果可能很嚴重，中國政府通常很快會宣稱，近14億中國人民的感情受到傷害。上海市互聯網信息辦公室表示，從今日起，將萬豪酒店的中文網站和中文版手機應用關閉一週。
Marriott said on its official account on Weibo, the Chinese social media platform, that “we will absolutely not support any separatist organization that will undermine China’s sovereignty and territorial integrity.”
萬豪在其設在中國社群媒體平台微博上的官方帳號中表示：「我們絕對不支持任何損害中國主權和領土完整的任何分裂組織。」
The Huangpu district of Shanghai, where Marriott China is based in, said on Wednesday that it has launched an investigation into the company for violating cybersecurity and advertising laws and questioned Marriott executives. Angry Chinese called for a boycott of the hotel chain.
萬豪中國總部所在地上海市黃浦區的政府週三表示，因公司涉嫌違反網路安全法和廣告法，已對其立案調查，政府還約談了萬豪高管。憤怒的中國人呼籲抵制這家連鎖酒店。
“They are earning our people’s money and yet they are thinking of splitting our motherland,” said a user on Weibo.
「他們掙的是我國人民的錢，想的卻是分裂我們的祖國，」一名微博上用戶這樣說。
In a statement, the company said it is fully cooperating with the investigation and has updated its website and apps to “ensure the proper country/region classifications.”
萬豪在一份聲明中表示，公司正全力配合調查，並已更新了其網站和應用程序，以「確保國家和地區劃分」的準確性。
“We sincerely apologize and thank guests and netizens for their candid feedback,” it said.
「我們再次真誠道歉，並感謝賓客以及網友的反饋。」
廣告
China is quick to lash out against any hint of support for an independent Tibet and for an independent Taiwan, a self-ruled democratic island that Beijing regards as a breakaway province and has vowed to take back by force if it declares independence. In recent years, many nationalistic Chinese have stoked a backlash against many foreign companies that are seen to be anti-China.
對於任何支持西藏獨立或台灣獨立的暗示，中國都會迅速做出反擊。北京認為，台灣這個民主自治的島嶼是從中國分裂出去一個省，並發誓說，如果台灣宣布獨立，將用武力將其收復。近年來，針對被視為反華的外國公司，許多民族主義的中國人發動了幾起的強烈抵制活動。
Last year, China forced the shutdown of more than 80 stores owned by the South Korean supermarket chain Lotte, which provided land for an American missile defense system intended to stop potential launches by North Korea.
去年，中國迫使經營樂天(Lotte)連鎖超市的韓國公司關閉了其在中國的80多家門店，因為這家公司為韓國安裝美國反導彈防禦系統提供了土地，該系統的目的是阻止朝鮮可能發射的導彈。
In 2008, hundreds of protesters called for a boycott of the French retailer Carrefour after Tibetan activists disrupted the Olympic torch’s journey through Paris. Irate Chinese internet users accused Carrefour of supporting pro-Tibetan independence groups.
2008年，西藏活動人士在巴黎擾亂了奧運火炬傳遞活動之後，數百名中國抗議者曾呼籲抵製法國零售商家樂福(Carrefour)。憤怒的中國網民指責家樂福支持藏獨團體。
In recent years, the government has condoned nationalist outbursts and then stamped them out after a short period.
在近幾年裡，雖然政府容忍了民族主義的爆發，但會在短期爆發後將其壓制下去。
In 2012, they allowed anti-Japanese demonstrations, which were fueled over islands that are claimed by both Japan and China. During those marches in Beijing, police officers stood by as eggs and plastic water bottles were thrown at the Japanese embassy. Protesters took photos using Canon cameras and ate from lunchboxes from Yoshinoya, a Japanese fast food chain, during their breaks.
2012年，中國和日本都聲稱擁有主權的島嶼糾紛引發了反日示威，政府允許了這些示威。在北京的遊行活動中，人們向日本大使館扔雞蛋和塑料瓶，警察袖手旁觀。抗議者中間休息時，用佳能(Canon)相機拍照，吃日本快餐連鎖店吉野家(Yoshinoya)的午餐盒飯。

黃瑞黎(Sui-Lee Wee)是《紐約時報》駐京記者。
