拜登商務部長提名人：將積極打擊中國貿易做法

Biden’s Commerce Pick Vows to Combat China and Climate Change

ANA SWANSON
2021年1月27日
WASHINGTON — Gina M. Raimondo, President Biden’s nominee to be the next commerce secretary, told lawmakers on Tuesday that she planned to help American communities bounce back from coronavirus, aggressively enforce trade rules to combat unfair Chinese practices and leverage the government’s power to mitigate climate change if confirmed.
華盛頓——拜登總統提名的下一任商務部長吉娜·M·雷蒙多(Gina M. Raimondo)週二對立法者說，如果得到確認，她計劃幫助美國社區從新冠病毒的打擊中重振旗鼓，積極執行貿易規則以打擊中國的不公平做法，並利用政府的權力減緩氣候變化。
Ms. Raimondo, the current governor of Rhode Island and a former venture capitalist, tried to reassure members of the Senate Commerce Committee that she would work with them on priorities like protecting American fisheries, expanding broadband access and promoting American research into cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence and advanced communications.
現任羅德島州州長、曾任風險投資家的雷蒙多試圖向參議院商務委員會(Senate Commerce Committee)成員保證，她將在保護美國漁業、擴大寬帶接入和促進美國對人工智慧和高級通信等尖端技術的研究等優先事項上，與他們合作。
“In this time of overlapping crises, the Commerce Department must be a partner to businesses and workers to help them innovate and grow,” Ms. Raimondo said.
雷蒙多說：「在這個危機重重的時代，商務部必須成為企業和工人的合作夥伴，幫助他們創新和成長。」
The Commerce Department has become increasingly powerful in recent years, given its wide-ranging authority over technology exports, climate change and other issues. Sometimes described as the government’s hall closet, the sprawling department is also responsible for enforcing trade rules, monitoring the weather and carrying out the census.
鑒於其在技術出口、氣候變化和其他問題上的廣泛權力，商務部的權力近年來已變得越來越大。這個龐大的部門有時被稱為政府大廳的壁櫥，它還負責執行貿易規則、監測天氣並進行人口普查。
廣告
The Trump administration made heavy use of the department’s authorities to crack down on Chinese technology firms, turning often to the so-called entity list, which allows the United States to block companies from selling American products and technology to certain foreign firms without first obtaining a license. Dozens of companies have been added to the Commerce Department’s list, including telecom giants like Huawei and ZTE, which many American lawmakers see as threats to national security.
川普政府大量利用該部門的權力來打擊中國的科技企業，動輒拿出所謂的實體名單，這使美國能夠阻止公司在未首先獲得執照的情況下向某些外國公司出售美國產品和技術。數十家企業已被列入美國商務部的名單，其中包括華為和中興等電信巨頭，許多美國立法者認為它們對國家安全構成威脅。
While lawmakers from both parties have flagged some Chinese companies as national security risks, Ms. Raimondo declined to commit to keeping Huawei or other Chinese firms on the entity list when pressed to do so by Senator Ted Cruz, Republican of Texas. She vowed to use the powers of the Commerce Department “to protect Americans and our network from Chinese interference,” adding, “that’s Huawei, ZTE, or any other company.”
雖然兩黨議員都將一些中國企業列為國家安全風險，但是，當得州共和黨參議員特德·克魯茲(Ted Cruz)催促雷蒙多將華為或其他中國企業保留在實體名單上時，雷蒙多拒絕作出這樣的承諾。她承諾要利用商務部的權力「保護美國人和我們的網路不受中國的干擾」，然後說，「指的是華為、中興或任何其他公司」。
The Biden administration is facing pressure from industry to relax some of the tight controls imposed on China by the Trump administration. On Monday, a semiconductor manufacturing group sent a letter to Ms. Raimondo urging her to review export controls that they said were harming the industry, and to impose multilateral controls in concert with allies instead.
拜登政府正面臨來自業界的壓力，它們要求其放鬆川普政府對中國施加的一些嚴格控制。週一，一個半導體製造集團致信雷蒙多，敦促她審查他們認為損害行業的出口管制，並與盟國一同實行多邊管制。
Ms. Raimondo called China’s actions “anti-competitive, hurtful to American workers and businesses” and said the country was “culpable for atrocious human rights abuses.”
雷蒙多稱中國的行為「是反競爭的，傷害了美國工人和企業」，並說中國「應該為嚴重侵犯人權的行為受到譴責」。
But her reluctance to explicitly commit to keeping certain Chinese companies on the entity list drew a sharp rebuke from some Republicans, including Mr. Cruz.
但是，她不願明確承諾將某些中國企業列入實體名單，這招致包括克魯茲在內的一些共和黨人的強烈譴責。
“There’s chatter in Washington that the Biden administration is contemplating going easy on China and removing companies from the entities list,” Mr. Cruz said during the hearing. “I certainly hope that does not happen because I think that would be profoundly contrary to the national security interest of the United States.”
「華盛頓有傳言，拜登政府正在考慮對中國採取寬鬆政策，並將那些公司從實體名單中刪除，」克魯茲在聽證會上說。「我當然希望這不會發生，因為我認為這將嚴重違背美國的國家安全利益。」
廣告
In a tweet on Tuesday, Representative Michael McCaul, Republican of Texas, said removing Huawei from the entity list would be a mistake, given the company’s links to the Chinese military and the threat it poses to American security.
得州共和黨眾議員麥可·麥考爾(Michael McCaul)在週二的一條推文中說，鑒於華為與中國軍方的聯繫，以及它對美國安全的威脅，將華為從實體名單中刪除是一個錯誤。
“It’s remarkable and frankly disturbing that Ms. Raimondo can’t commit to keeping #Huawei on the Entity List,” he said. “I strongly urge her to reconsider.”
「雷蒙多女士不能承諾將#華為列為實體名單，這令人驚訝，坦率地說也令人不安，」他說。「我強烈促請她重新考慮。」
Ms. Raimondo also appeared to walk a careful line on the steel and aluminum tariffs that President Donald J. Trump levied on foreign countries, including many in Europe.
雷蒙多似乎還對唐納德·J·川普總統對包括許多歐洲國家在內的外國徵收鋼鐵和鋁關稅的做法，採取了謹慎的態度。
She declined to say whether the Biden administration would remove or change the metal tariffs, instead committing it to carrying out a broad review of trade policies in consultation with its allies and pursuing uncompetitive trade behavior from China. She also pledged to ensure that the process that allows certain companies to avoid the steel and aluminum tariffs — which faced criticism during the Trump administration for being complicated and arbitrary — was swift, fair and objective.
她拒絕透露拜登政府是否會取消或改變金屬關稅，而是承諾與盟友協商，對貿易政策進行廣泛審查，並追查中國迴避正當競爭的貿易行為。她還承諾確保允許某些公司免徵鋼鐵和鋁關稅的程序迅速、公平和客觀。在川普政府執政期間，這些關稅因為複雜、武斷而受到批評。
In a sign of how broad a portfolio she would be overseeing, Ms. Raimondo vowed to focus on a wide range of other issues, including promoting the country’s capacity in advanced technologies like artificial intelligence, blockchain and 5G telecommunications, and making broadband access, particularly in rural areas and tribal lands, “a very top priority the minute I get into the job.”
雷蒙多誓言要集中精力解決一系列廣泛問題，由此可見她的職責多麼寬泛，包括提高美國在人工智慧、區塊鏈和5G電信等先進技術方面的能力，以及寬帶接入的普及，特別是在農村地區和部落土地上，「這是我上任後的首要優先事項。」
She described internet access as a “necessity” in the pandemic, pointing to how it allows children to take online classes, patients to visit with their doctors and people to sign up to receive the coronavirus vaccine.
她將上網描述為大流行中的「必需品」，指出互聯網如何讓兒童能夠上網路課程，讓患者去看醫生，以及讓人們報名接種新冠疫苗。
廣告
“It’s not a luxury good,” she said. “Everybody deserves access to broadband.”
「這不是奢侈品，」她說。「每個人都有權用到寬帶。」
Several senators praised her combination of public and private sector experience, saying those skills could help the country deal with economic damage from the pandemic, increase investment in American workers and business, and promote the marine and space economies, which the Commerce Department also oversees.
幾位參議員讚許了她在公共和私營部門的雙重經驗，稱這些技能可以幫助美國應對大流行造成的經濟損失，增加對美國工人和企業的投資，並促進由商務部監管的海洋和太空經濟的發展。
Ms. Raimondo said that her background as a state official and a venture capitalist before that had prepared her to help realize the department’s mission. She talked about the effects of offshoring and factory closures on her family in the 1980s, when her father lost his job at a local watch factory and was forced into early retirement.
雷蒙多說，她曾擔任州官員和風險資本家的背景為她幫助實現該部門的使命做好了準備。她談到1980年代離岸外包和工廠關閉對她的家庭的影響，當時她的父親在當地一家錶廠失業，被迫提前退休。
During the hearing, Ms. Raimondo focused on her role in bringing down Rhode Island’s unemployment rate, once the highest in the nation, crediting work force training and small business lending programs that she helped initiate. Rhode Island’s unemployment rate sat at 6.6 percent when Ms. Raimondo took office as governor in 2015. It then fell fairly steadily to a low of 3.4 percent at the beginning of 2020, mirroring a broader trend around the United States. The jobless rate has since spiked in the wake of the pandemic, and in November it sat at 7.3 percent, the 12th highest of any state in the nation.
在聽證會上，雷蒙多將重點放在她在降低羅德島州的失業率中扮演的角色（那裡曾是美國失業率最高的地方），歸功於她幫助發起的勞動力培訓和小企業貸款計劃。當雷蒙多於2015年就任州長時，羅德島州的失業率達到6.6％。後來，失業率穩步下降，在2020年初降至3.4％的低點，與美國各地的廣泛趨勢相呼應。自從大流行之後，失業率飆升，羅德島州的數據在11月達到7.3％，在美國所有州中排名第12位。
