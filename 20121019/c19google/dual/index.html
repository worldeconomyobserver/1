谷歌发布第三季度财报股价大跌

Shares Fall Sharply as Google Earnings Are Released Early

CLAIRE CAIN MILLER
2012年10月19日
SAN FRANCISCO — Google shocked investors Thursday when it mistakenly filed its earnings report with the Securities and Exchange Commission several hours earlier than planned, reporting disappointing third-quarter profits.
旧金山——本周四， 谷歌公司(Google) 向美国证券交易委员会（Securities and Exchange Commission，简称SEC）呈交财务报告，称第三季度的利润令人失望。因为公司操作失误，呈交报告的时间比原计划提前了几个小时，让投资者震惊不已。
Google stock dropped sharply after the report, down more than 9 percent, or $68 a share, before Nasdaq halted trading in its shares in the early afternoon. Shares resumed trading late in the day, and ended off 8 percent.
报告公布之后，谷歌股票大幅下跌，在纳斯达克(Nasdaq)于昨日午后不久停止其交易之前，跌幅超过了9%，或每股68美元；交易于当天晚些时候恢复，收盘时跌幅为8%。
The mistaken filing surprised stock analysts, reporters and even Google’s own public relations staff, which did not know about it at first. Google later blamed the error on R.R. Donnelley & Sons, the publisher, which it said had filed an early draft of the earnings “without authorization.”
呈交报告时间的操作失误震惊了股票分析人士和记者，甚至包括谷歌自己的公关人员，后者一开始并不知晓此事。谷歌随后归咎于发布报告的R.R.当纳利(R.R. Donnelley & Sons)公司，称后者发布的是一份“未经授权”的财务报告前期草稿。
R.R. Donnelley, whose shares dipped as much as 5.6 percent before recovering, said in a statement that it had started an investigation to determine how the error happened.
R.R.当纳利的股价在下跌多达5.6%之后才开始反弹，该公司在声明中表示，已经开始调查错误发生的原因。
The earnings release was such an early draft that it said “pending Larry quote” at the top where Google planned to insert a quote from Mr. Page. 
公诸于世的财务报告的确是一份相当前期的草稿，以至于报告顶端还写着“此处拟引用拉里发言”，意思是谷歌计划在那个地方插入一段拉里·佩奇(Larry Page)的发言。
The company reported third-quarter revenue of $14.1 billion from both Google’s core search business and from Motorola Mobility, the ailing cellphone maker that Google recently acquired. This was an increase of 45 percent over the year-ago quarter. 
谷歌公司报告的第三季度收入为141亿美元，涵盖了谷歌的核心搜索业务，以及谷歌不久前收购的境况不佳的手机制造商摩托罗拉移动公司(Motorola Mobility)。这比去年同期增长了45%。
But net income sank to $2.18 billion, or $6.53 a share, from $2.73 billion, or $8.33 a share. Analysts largely blamed losses from Motorola and a decline in Google ad prices for the poor results.
然而，报告中的净收益从27.3亿美元跌至21.8亿美元，亦即从每股8.33美元跌至每股6.53美元。分析人士认为，业绩不良的主要原因是摩托罗拉的亏损和谷歌广告价格的下跌。
On a conference call with Wall Street analysts, nonetheless, Google executives described it as a strong quarter and addressed concerns that an increase in mobile usage and advertising has led to a decline in ad revenue.
尽管如此，在与华尔街分析师的电话会议中，谷歌高管称这个季度业绩良好，并对手机应用和手机广告增多导致其广告收入下降的担忧作出了回应 。
Google was on track to generate $8 billion in the coming year from mobile, including ads and sales of devices, ads and content in the Google Play store, the company said. It did not break down how much of that would come from advertising, but said it was the vast majority.
谷歌称，公司有望在未来一年从手机业务中获得80亿美元收入，收入来源包括手机广告和手机销售，以及Google Play商店的广告和商品。谷歌没有具体说明广告收入将占多大比例，只是说，收入的绝大部分会来自广告。
“Monetization on mobile queries right now is a significant fraction of desktop,” said Larry Page, Google’s chief executive. He said Google was exploring new ways to make more money as people increasingly use phones and tablets instead of desktop computers, and said it was “uniquely positioned to get through that transition and to profit from it.”
“目前，手机查询带来的收入是一块大蛋糕。”谷歌首席执行官拉里·佩奇说。他说，谷歌正在探索赚更多钱的新方法，因为与台式电脑相比，人们用手机或平板电脑用得越来越多。他还说，谷歌“拥有独特的优势，能够承受这一转变，并从中获利” 。
Later in the day, Google filed an amended earnings report with the S.E.C.
当天晚些时候，谷歌向SEC提交了订正的财务报告。
Both net revenue and earnings were well below analysts’ expectations. The miss was all the more surprising because Google shares had an outstanding quarter, increasing 35 percent over the last three months, in part because of stumbles by competitors like Facebook. This month, Google’s market capitalization had edged ahead of Microsoft’s to make it second most valuable tech company, behind Apple, but it has again fallen behind Microsoft.
报告中的净收入和利润数字远远低于分析人士的预期。更让这种糟糕业绩显得不可思议的是，谷歌股票曾在此前一个季度创下涨幅35%的辉煌，部分是因为Facebook之类的竞争对手跌了跟头。本月，谷歌的市值曾一度小幅领先于微软(Microsoft)，由此成为价值排名第二的科技公司，仅次于苹果公司(Apple)。不过，谷歌后来再次落到了微软之下。
Before the recent rally, the shares had been held back by concerns over antitrust and privacy investigations by regulators in the United States and elsewhere as well as concerns about Motorola’s losses and the potential of new Google businesses in the face of slowing search advertising growth.
近期的反弹之前，谷歌的股价曾受到抑制，原因是种种因素引起了人们的担忧，包括美国和其他地方的监管机构对谷歌进行的反垄断和侵犯隐私调查、摩托罗拉的亏损、谷歌新业务的潜力，以及搜索广告增长放缓的局面。
Under the leadership of Mr. Page, Google’s chief executive since last year, the company has gradually ameliorated fears about Google’s alternate sources of revenue.
在去年上任的首席执行官佩奇的领导下，谷歌已经逐渐减少了人们对其替代收入来源的担忧。
Long the leader in search advertising, with 75 percent market share, it has now become the leader in both other forms of digital advertising, display and mobile, according to eMarketer, the digital advertising research firm. Its market share in display is 15 percent, putting it ahead of Yahoo and Facebook, and its share of mobile ad revenue is 55 percent.
据数字广告研究企业eMarketer称，作为在搜索广告市场拥有75%份额的长期领头羊，谷歌目前已然成为另外两种数字广告的市场领军者，即展示广告和手机广告。谷歌在展示广告市场的份额是15%，领先于雅虎(Yahoo)和Facebook，手机广告收入则占市场份额的55%。
But other concerns about Google’s business have been amplified.
然而，对于谷歌业务的其他担忧已经越来越大了。
It was the first quarter that Motorola Mobility has been included in full-quarter earnings, and its performance weighed on Google’s results. It lost $527 million in the quarter, despite deep cuts and layoffs.
这是谷歌将摩托罗拉移动公司列入其整季度盈利报告的第一个季度，而摩托罗拉的表现拖累了谷歌的结果。尽管实施了大量的削减和裁员，摩托罗拉仍旧在这一季度亏损了5.27亿美元。
“Motorola is a millstone, and as revenue declined, expenses remained,” said Colin Gillis, an analyst at BGC Partners.
BGC Partners的分析师科林·吉利斯(Colin Gillis)说，“摩托罗拉是一个重担，收入下降，成本却依然故我。”
Google also suffered from plummeting prices for clicks on Google ads for the fourth consecutive quarter. The average cost per click decreased 3 percent from last quarter and 15 percent from last year, a steeper decline than analysts expected, in part because of the growth of mobile ads, which tend to cost less.
与此同时，按点击量计算的广告价格连续四个季度暴跌也让谷歌蒙受了损失。按点击量计算的价格比上一季度降低了3%，比去年降低了15%，下跌幅度超过了分析人士的预期，部分原因是手机广告的增长，后者的价格往往更低。
“All of these mobile devices are generating clicks that are just less valuable to advertisers,” Mr. Gillis said.
“大量的手机设备为广告主带来了成本更低的点击量。”吉利斯说。
