數據顯示中國經濟增速觸底企穩

Slowdown in China Appears to Have Ended, for Now

BETTINA WASSENER
2012年10月19日
HONG KONG — After months of slowing growth, the Chinese economy may finally have bottomed out.
香港——經過數月的增長放緩，中國的經濟可能已最終見底。
Data released Thursday from China offered some surprisingly positive news on economic performance in September, just as the country prepares for a once-in-a-decade leadership transition.
中國在周四公布的數據顯示，正當該國為十年一次的領導層換屆作準備之時，其9月的經濟狀況有出人意料的良好表現。
Industrial output grew 9.2 percent, compared with a year earlier, and retail sales expanded 14.2 percent, the statistics office in Beijing said. Both figures beat expectations and improved on the expansions in August.
國家統計局表示，和去年同期相比，中國9月份的工業產值增加了9.2%，零售銷售額增加了14.2%。這兩個數據都高於預期，在8月的增長基礎上又有所改善。
Similarly, investment in fixed assets rose 20.5 percent in the first nine months this year from the comparable period in 2011, slightly better than analysts had expected, showing that stimulus measures announced by Beijing over the past year have gradually put a floor under the economy’s deceleration.
同樣地，和去年同期相比，今年前九個月的固定資產投資增加了20.5%，略高於分析人士預期，這表明北京在過去一年中出台的刺激措施對經濟減速逐漸起到了穩定作用。
During the July-to-September quarter as a whole, gross domestic product expanded 7.4 percent, compared with a year earlier, a figure that represented the slowest pace of growth since early 2009, when the global economy was reeling from the financial crisis.
從7月到9月的第三季度，國內生產總值與去年同期相比增加了7.4%，這是自2009年年初以來最低的增長速度，當時的全球經濟在金融危機的衝擊下徘徊不前。
The figure was in line with expectations, and it confirmed what analysts and executives from across a wide range of sectors had been saying for much of this year: that the heady days of double-digit expansion in China are a thing of the past and that the economy has slowed markedly this year.
這一數據符合預期，數據也證實了各不同行業分析人士和企業高管今年以來一直的論說：中國呈兩位數增長的輝煌時期已一去不復返，今年的經濟增長已顯著放緩。
Weak overseas demand for Chinese exports and government policies aimed at combating inflation and soaring property prices have combined to slow the once red-hot pace of expansion. The picture for September appeared to show that the slowdown has, at least for now, come to an end.
中國出口商品的疲軟海外需求、以及旨在遏制通脹和高昂房產價格的政府政策等因素共同造成曾經一度白熱化的增長放緩。9月的經濟形勢似乎表明，經濟放緩已經結束，至少目前是這樣。
“Clearly, concerns over continued slowdown can now be put to rest,” Dariusz Kowalczyk, an analyst at Crédit Agricole in Hong Kong, said in a research note. The data, he added, confirm “that growth is picking up and that China is not at risk of hard landing.”
“顯而易見，關於經濟持續放緩的擔憂可以暫時平息，”法國農業信貸銀行(Crédit Agricole)駐香港的分析師戴銳斯·科瓦茨(Dariusz Kowalczyk)在一份研究紀要中寫道。他還指出，這些數據證實“增長速度正在加快，中國沒有硬着陸的風險。”
Zhiwei Zhang, a China economist at Nomura in Hong Kong, said the data sent “very positive signals” and helped “reinforce our view that growth will rebound visibly” in the fourth quarter.
野村證券(Nomura)駐香港的中國經濟學家張智威說，數據發出了“很積極的信號”，並幫助“強化了我們的這一觀點，即增長將會在第四季度顯著回升。” 
Analysts said a combination of slightly improved global circumstances and local policy measures had helped end the slide in growth.
分析人士指出，略有改善的全球形勢和國內的政策措施幫助終止了增長率的下滑。
Trade statistics released last Saturday showed that Chinese exports had risen 9.9 percent in September, compared with a year earlier, much more rapidly than analysts had expected, as shipments to the United States and Southeast Asia had improved.
上周六公布的外貿統計數據顯示，由於中國對美國和東南亞的出口量增加，中國9月的出口額與去年同期相比上升了9.9%，增速大大超過分析家預期。
At the same time, stimulus measures have finally begun to bear fruit. Eager not to set off an investment binge and spike in property prices of the kind that followed a huge stimulus program after the 2008 crisis, the Chinese authorities have taken a much more measured approach to bolstering flagging growth this time.
同時，刺激措施最終開始生效。為了不像2008年金融危機之後出台的大規模刺激計劃那樣再次引起投資熱潮和房價飆升，中國當局此次採取了更為慎重的方法來提振萎靡的經濟增長。
Stimulus steps have come in the form of smaller steps, like loosened lending constraints for banks, increased approvals for infrastructure projects and two small interest rate cuts, which appear to have stopped the slowdown for now, analysts said.
這次的刺激措施採取了較小的步驟，例如放鬆對銀行的放貸限制、增加對基礎設施工程的核准、以及兩次小額降息，分析家稱，這些措施似乎暫時阻止了經濟放緩形勢。
Despite signs of renewed growth in sectors like transportation, exports and tourism, many economists said that the economy was likely to accelerate only modestly in the coming quarters, and that it remained vulnerable to the fall-out from the euro zone crisis and the looming “fiscal cliff” of automatic tax increases and spending cuts in the United States.
儘管在運輸、出口和旅遊等領域出現了增長復蘇的跡象，很多經濟學家稱，在未來幾個季度內，中國經濟增長的速度不會太顯著，而且在歐元區危機的負面影響下，加上美國“財政懸崖”（自動增稅和削減開支）陰影的逼近，中國經濟依然脆弱。
Moreover, the Chinese property sector remains weak. Figures released Thursday showed that prices for new homes in 70 major cities slipped 1.3 percent in September, compared with a year earlier. Some analysts worry about the financial health of developers that overinvested amid the tide of liquidity that accompanied the 2008-9 stimulus rush.
另外，中國的房地產行業依然脆弱。周四發佈的數據顯示，和去年同期相比，中國70個大城市的新房價格在9月下降了1.3%。一些分析家擔心開發商的財務健康狀況，這些開發商在2008-9年刺激計劃帶來的流動性資金浪潮推動下投資過度。
The policy outlook also remains murky, with little clarity as to how China’s coming leadership will shape the country’s efforts to put its economy on a firmer economic footing.
政策前景也依然不明朗，中國的新一屆領導人將會採取哪些措施來為其經濟打下更堅固的基礎還屬未知。
“As it stands, the ‘ups’ match the ‘downs,”’ said Alistair Thornton, an economist at IHS Global Insight in Beijing. “The risks of a hard landing are narrowing by the day, but it is too early to suggest that a recovery is a given.”
“從目前情況來看，好壞摻半，”全球信息公司IHS Global Insight駐北京的經濟學家阿利斯泰爾•桑頓(Alistair Thornton)說。“硬着陸的風險正日漸消除，但要說復蘇必定會出現也為時過早。”
Longer term, he said, there was a risk that financial markets could be disappointed by the lingering lack of clarity over China’s economic policy direction. The markets will be looking for signs of policy direction and the central reforms that will be needed to rebalance the economy, he said, but “it may take weeks if not months for things to become clearer.”
他說，從長期來看有一個風險，就是中國經濟政策方向揮之不去的不明朗，可能會讓金融市場失望。他說，市場將會尋找政策方向信號和平衡經濟所需的中央改革信號，但“事情可能要經過數周、甚至數月之後才會更加明確。”
