中国将合并银监会与保监会，加强共产党控制

China’s Communist Party Consolidates Power Over Financial Sector

KEITH BRADSHER, 储百亮
2018年3月13日
BEIJING — The Chinese government said on Tuesday that it planned to overhaul supervision of the country’s debt-ridden financial sector, its environmental regulators and other essential government agencies in a broad move intended to further consolidate the Communist Party’s hold on official levers of power.
北京——中国政府周二宣布计划，全面改革对其负债累累的金融行业的监管，以及环境监管机构和另外一些至关重要的政府机构。这项广泛的行动旨在进一步巩固共产党对官方权力杠杆的控制。
Premier Li Keqiang asked the National People’s Congress, the country’s legislature, to approve a plan that would combine China’s banking and insurance regulators in an effort to bolster their ability to monitor financial institutions. At the same time, both agencies would relinquish some of their broad policy responsibilities to China’s central bank, which would acquire an even greater role in preserving financial stability in what is now the world’s second-largest economy.
总理李克强请求中国的立法机构全国人民代表大会批准一个方案，其内容是合并中国的银行业和保险业监管机构，以增强其监管金融机构的能力。与此同时，这两个机构还会将各自广泛的政策职责中的一部分移交给中国的央行。后者将在保持现已是世界第二大经济体的中国的金融稳定方面发挥更重要的作用。
The plan, which is set for essentially automatic approval by the legislature in the coming days, would also take many responsibilities away from the National Development and Reform Commission, a bastion of central economic planning and heavy industry, and from a variety of other, older commissions and bureaus often perceived as cozy with the industries they regulate.
这个方案将在未来几天获得人大批准，获批基本上是必然的。它还会削减国家发展和改革委员会，以及其他很多通常被认为与所监管行业关系亲密的老资格委员会及政府部委的大量职权。其中，国家发展和改革委员会是中央经济规划和重工业的堡垒。
The moves could consolidate bureaucracies that have long overlapped, and at times clashed, hindering effective policy.
这些举措可能会合并一些长期重叠，有时甚至相互冲突，阻碍有效政策的官僚机构。
But more broadly, the proposals are part of a plan that President Xi Jinping laid out last month aimed at strengthening the top-down control of the Communist Party. The plan released on Monday focused on government reorganization, but Mr. Xi and other officials have stressed that any changes are intended to strengthen, rather than dilute, party control.
但更广泛地说，这些提议属于中国国家主席习近平上月阐述的一项方案，旨在加强共产党自上而下的控制。周一公布的该方案把重点放在了政府重组上，但习近平和另一些官员强调，任何改变都是为了加强而不是削弱党的控制。
Liu He, President Xi’s right-hand man in overseeing the economy and financial system, outlined the party’s central role in the reorganization in a lengthy statement published on Tuesday morning in People’s Daily, the official mouthpiece of the Chinese Communist Party. Mr. Liu, an economist, was promoted to the party’s Politburo last year.
周二上午，中国共产党的官方喉舌《人民日报》发表了一篇长文。习近平在经济和金融体系监管方面的左膀右臂刘鹤在文章中概述了党在政府重组过程中的核心作用。去年，身为经济专家的刘鹤被提拔为政治局委员。
“Strengthening the party’s overall leadership is the core issue,” he wrote, alluding to a famous Mao Zedong saying. “In the party, government, military, and civil sciences, in the east, west, south, north and center, the party leads all.”
 “加强党的全面领导是核心问题，”他写道，间接提到了毛泽东的一句名言。“党政军民学，东西南北中，党是领导一切的。”
In other changes, control of the country’s forests grasslands and waterways would be given to a new superministry for natural resources. The existing environmental protection agency would be broadened into a larger ministry for ecological and environmental protection.
其他改革包括，中国森林草原和水路的管控权将交给一个新成立的超级自然资源部。现有的环保部将被扩充为更大的生态和环境保护部。
The plan unveiled on Tuesday calls for merging the China Banking Regulatory Commission and the China Insurance Regulatory Commission. But it falls short of widely discussed plans over the past few years of the possible creation of a financial super-regulator that would also include China’s central bank and the China Securities Regulatory Commission.
周二公布的方案要求合并中国银行业监督管理委员会和中国保险监督管理委员会。但它没有提到过去几年里讨论甚广的方案，即成立一个包括央行和中国证券监督管理委员会在内的超级金融监管机构。
The securities regulator oversees the country’s stock markets and competes for jurisdiction with the other banking and insurance agencies for oversight of a large gray area of investments that combine aspects of debt and equity. The central bank seeks to maintain the overall stability of the financial system.
中国证券监督管理委员会监管着中国的股市，并与另一些银行和保险管理机构争夺对一个巨大的灰色投资领域的管辖权。这个灰色领域结合了部分债务和股权。央行寻求保持金融体系的整体稳定。
The intellectual justification for the merger of just the banking and insurance regulators, Chinese experts said in interviews over the last two weeks, is to create a single regulator for financial institutions that accept money from the public and invest it with guaranteed rates of return. But the China Securities Regulatory Commission needed to stay separate from the new banking and insurance agency, they suggested, because its role involves overseeing markets, in which customers know that they are taking a risk that they may not recover their investments.
中国专家在过去两周接受采访时表示，只合并银行和保险监管机构的合理性是成立一个单一的监管者，监督那些接受公众资金，并用其进行回报率有保障的投资的金融机构。但他们表示，中国证券监督管理委员会须与新成立的银行保险机构保持独立，因为该委员会的职责涉及监管市场，而市场客户知道自己在冒险，可能无法收回投资。
But some Western experts have tended to recommend a broader combination of regulatory agencies. They have warned that Chinese financial institutions like Anbang Insurance Group, recently taken over by the central government after it embarked on a program of feverish overseas investment, have tended to exploit gaps in regulatory responsibility between the agencies.
不过，一些西方专家倾向于建议将这些监管机构更多地组合起来。他们曾警告称，安邦保险集团等中国金融机构曾经试图利用这些机构之间的监管责任空白。安邦在启动了一个狂热的海外投资项目之后，前不久被中国中央政府接管。
Mr. Xi did create a lightly staffed financial supervisory commission last summer, which Mr. Liu is expected to take charge of in the coming days, that oversees all the regulatory agencies. Alicia Garcia-Herrero, an economist in the Hong Kong office of Natixis, a French financial institution, said that the limited financial regulatory consolidation set forth on Tuesday would leave that commission with a lot of power.
去年夏天，习近平设立了一个人员较少的金融监管委员会，预计刘鹤未来会担任该委员会的负责人。法国金融机构Natixis驻香港办公室的经济学家艾丽西亚·加西亚-埃雷罗(Alicia Garcia-Herrero)表示，周二提出的有限的金融监管机构的合并将使该委员会拥有很大的权力。
“The beauty of this more fragmented model for Xi is that he is still the final overseer through the oversight commission he created,” she said.
“对习近平来说，这个更加支离破碎的模式的好处在于，他依然可以通过他设立的监管委员会进行最终的监管，”她说。
The restructuring will also create a new, separate National Market Regulation and Administration Bureau to take over responsibility for registering and supervising companies, food and drug inspection, and price supervision. The new bureau will also grab control over antimonopoly actions from the Ministry of Commerce and another, smaller government agency.
重组还将设立一个新的、独立的国家市场监督管理总局，负责公司注册和监管、食品和药品审查，以及价格监督。这个新部委还将从商务部以及另一个规模较小的政府机构手中拿来对反垄断行动的控制权。
China’s industrial takeoff and feverish urban growth have created dangerous levels of air, water and soil pollution, and placed deepening strains on land and water resources. The reforms proposed creating two superministries to take on those problems: a Ministry of Natural Resources and a Ministry of Ecology and Environment.
中国的工业腾飞和狂热的城市发展导致空气、水和土壤污染达到了危险水平，给土地资源和水资源带来了日益严重的压力。改革提议设立两个超级部门，来解决这些问题：自然资源部和生态环境部。
The Ministry of Natural Resources will gain power over urban and township planning, as well as management of water, grasslands, forests, wetlands, and maritime resources.
自然资源部将获得授权，对城市和乡镇规划以及水源、草原、森林、湿地和海洋资源进行管理。
The new Ministry for Ecology and Environment will take over responsibility for climate change and greenhouse emissions policies, which were previous under the National Development and Reform Commission. The new environmental super-ministry will also take over antipollution tasks previously handled by the ministries of land and of water resources, leaving them further weakened.
新成立的生态环境部将负责制订气候变化和温室气体排放政策，这些政策之前是由国家发展和改革委员会负责制订的。新的超级环境部门将接管之前由国土资源部和水利部承担的反污染任务，导致后两个部门的权力进一步被削弱。

储百亮(Chris Buckley)是《世界经济观察报》驻京记者。
