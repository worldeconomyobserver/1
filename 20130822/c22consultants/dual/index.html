英国咨询公司总监在上海被捕，疑与葛兰素案有关

Police in China Arrest British Executive

DAVID BARBOZA
2013年8月22日
SHANGHAI — The police in Shanghai have arrested a British investigator who specialized in advising foreign investors on fraud, cheating and other business risks in China, a spokeswoman for the British Embassy in Beijing said on Wednesday.
上海——驻北京的英国大使馆一名发言人周三称，上海警方已逮捕一名英国籍调查员，他专为外国投资者提供针对中国的造假、欺诈以及其他商业风险的咨询。
The investigator, Peter Humphrey, has been held by the Shanghai police since early July. He is managing director of ChinaWhys, a risk management consulting firm that has done work for GlaxoSmithKline, the British pharmaceutical group that is facing graft and bribery allegations in China, raising speculation that his detention might be linked to that case.
这位名叫韩飞龙(Peter Humphrey)的调查员自7月初以来一直被上海警方拘留。他是风险管理咨询公司中慧(ChinaWhys)公司的总裁，这家公司曾为英国制药集团葛兰素史克(GlaxoSmithKline)提供过咨询服务，目前葛兰素史克正面临中国的贿赂指控，这引起人们对他被逮捕可能与贿赂指控有关的推测。
A spokeswoman for the British Embassy, Hannah Oussedik, would say only that he had been formally arrested. “We can confirm the arrest of a British national, Peter Humphrey, in Shanghai, China, on Monday, 19th August,” Ms. Oussedik said in a brief telephone interview. “We are providing consular assistance to the family.”
英国驻华大使馆发言人汉娜·乌西迪克(Hannah Oussedik)只说韩飞龙已被正式逮捕。乌西迪克在一次简短的电话采访中说，“我们可以确认，英国人韩飞龙于8月19日周一在中国上海被逮捕。我们正在为其家人提供领事协助。”
The Wall Street Journal, which first reported Mr. Humphrey’s arrest, said that his wife, Yu Yingzeng, was also formally arrested in Shanghai, and that the couple are both accused of illegally purchasing information about people. Ms. Oussedik could not confirm those details. Ms. Yu is an American citizen. U.S. officials in Beijing and Shanghai declined to comment Wednesday.
最先报道韩飞龙被捕的《华尔街日报》(The Wall Street Journal)称，他的妻子于英增（音译）也在上海被正式逮捕，夫妻两人均被指控非法购买有关他人的信息。乌西迪克不能证实这些细节。于英增是美籍华人。在北京和上海的美国官员周三拒绝对此事发表评论。
The formal arrest means the police have more time to pursue their investigation of the couple before deciding to drop the case or seek an indictment.
正式逮捕意味着，在决定是放弃这个案子、还是对他们提起诉讼之前，警方有更多时间从事对这对夫妇的调查。
China’s rough-and-tumble commercial environment, in which laws and rules are often arbitrarily enforced or ignored, can make for risky work in private corporate investigations. But Mr. Humphrey, a former journalist for Reuters, had worked in the area for over a decade. He co-founded ChinaWhys in 2003, and his wife, Ms. Yu, is a co-founder and general manager, according to the company’s Web site.
中国乱七八糟的商业环境给私营公司调查带来不少风险，这个环境中法律法规经常被任意执行或被忽视。然而，曾任路透社(Reuters)记者的韩飞龙已在这个行业干了十多年。根据中慧公司网站上的介绍，韩飞龙在2003年与妻子于英增一起合伙成立了中慧公司，于英增是这家公司的总经理。
One person who knows Mr. Humphrey and who asked not to be identified said he appears to have been arrested in connection with his work for GlaxoSmithKline.
一名认识韩飞龙的人说，韩飞龙被逮捕似乎与他为葛兰素史克做的工作有关，这个人要求不具名。
The government has detained four of the drugmaker’s Chinese executives in connection with an investigation into bribery and tax fraud. GlaxoSmithKline distanced itself from some of the former executives after investigators said the executives confessed to fraud.
中国政府已逮捕了这家制药集团的四名中国籍高管，都与对公司行贿和逃税的调查有关。调查人员称这些高管已坦白从事欺诈，这之后，葛兰素史克与其中一些前高管划清了界限。
Officials at multinational corporations operating in China have complained in recent weeks that the Chinese government is using law enforcement to weaken international competitors in commercial sectors where Chinese companies are not strong or have spent very little money to develop their own products.
最近几周在中国运营的跨国公司高管抱怨说，中国政府利用执法来削弱一些商业领域中的国际竞争者，在这些领域中，中国公司不强大或是投入很少的资金开发自己的产品。
“It’s a hugely political thing. They’re just using him as a lever to force the pharmaceutical industry to lower prices,” said the person who knows Mr. Humphrey.
那位认识韩飞龙的知情人士说，“这件事极具政治性。他们是在以他为杠杆，来迫使整个制药行业降价。”
The person added that although Mr. Humphrey had worked for GlaxoSmithKline, it was not clear whether Mr. Humphrey was doing so at the time of his detention last month.
该人士补充说，虽然韩飞龙以前为葛兰素史克工作过，但上月他在被拘留时是否仍在这么做还不清楚。
The detention of Mr. Humphrey and his wife is even more politically sensitive because his wife, a naturalized American citizen, is part of a well-connected Chinese family. She is the daughter of a prominent Chinese scientist who played a central role in the development of China’s nuclear weapons program.
韩飞龙和他妻子被拘捕一事，因他已加入美籍的妻子的家庭背景，而更具政治敏感性。她的父亲是一个对中国核武器项目发展起过关键作用的科学家。

储百亮(Chris Buckley)和Keith Bradsher自香港对本文有报道贡献。
