蓋茲夫婦離婚案：巨額財富怎麼分？

The Gateses’ Public Split Spotlights a Secretive Fortune

NICHOLAS KULISH, DAVID GELLES, ANUPREETA DAS, KATE KELLY
2021年5月14日
The fortune of Bill Gates and Melinda French Gates exceeds the size of Morocco’s annual economy, combines the value of Ford, Twitter and Marriott International and is triple the endowment of Harvard. While few know how their wealth will be divided in the divorce, one thing is clear: breaking it up can’t be easy.
比爾·蓋茲(Bill Gates)和梅琳達·弗蘭奇·蓋茲(Melinda French Gates)的財富超過了摩洛哥的全年經濟總額，是福特(Ford)、Twitter和萬豪國際(Marriott International)市值的總和，是哈佛大學捐贈基金的三倍。雖然沒什麼人知道他們離婚後會如何分割財富，但很顯然：分割這些財富不是一件容易的事。
Mr. Gates built one of the great fortunes in human history when he founded Microsoft in 1975 with Paul Allen. The Gateses’ net worth is estimated to be more than $124 billion, and includes assets as varied as trophy real estate, public company stocks and rare artifacts.
比爾·蓋茲於1975年與保羅·艾倫(Paul Allen)共同創立了微軟(Microsoft)公司，藉此積累了人類歷史上最為龐大的財富之一。蓋茲的凈資產估計超過1240億美元，其中包括從頂級房產、上市公司股票到稀有藝術品的各種資產。
There’s a big stake in the luxury Four Seasons hotel chain. There are hundreds of thousands of acres of farmland and ranch land, including Buffalo Bill’s historic Wyoming ranch. There are billions of dollars’ worth of shares in companies like AutoNation and Waste Management. There’s a beachfront mansion in Southern California. And one of Leonardo da Vinci’s notebooks.
他是豪華酒店連鎖四季(Four Seasons)的大股東，有數十萬英畝的農田和牧場，其中包括歷史悠久的水牛比爾(Buffalo Bill)的懷俄明州牧場。擁有諸如AutoNation和廢棄物管理公司(Waste Management)之類的企業數十億美元的股份。在南加州有一所海邊豪宅。還有列奧納多·達·芬奇(Leonardo da Vinci)的一個筆記本。
“The amount of money and the diversity of assets that are involved in this divorce boggles the imagination,” said David Aronson, a lawyer who has represented wealthy clients in divorce cases. “There have rarely been cases that are even close to this in size.”
「這場離婚所涉及的金錢和資產的多樣性令人難以想像，」為富有客戶代理離婚案件的律師戴維·阿隆森(David Aronson)說。「幾乎沒有案例能接近這個規模。」
廣告
Only the 2019 divorce between the Amazon founder Jeff Bezos and his now ex-wife, the novelist and philanthropist MacKenzie Scott, was bigger. Mr. Bezos had an estimated fortune of $137 billion, though mostly in Amazon stock, and Ms. Scott kept 4 percent of Amazon’s shares, worth $36 billion at the time.
只有一個例外，涉及的資產價值還要更多，那就是2019年亞馬遜創始人傑夫·貝佐斯(Jeff Bezos)和他現在的前妻、小說家兼慈善家麥肯琪·史考特(MacKenzie Scott)的離婚案。貝佐斯的財富估計為1370億美元，儘管大部分是亞馬遜股票，史考特保留了亞馬遜4％的股份，當時價值360億美元。
But Mr. Gates has for decades been diversifying his holdings; he owns just 1.3 percent of Microsoft. Instead, his stock portfolio includes stakes in dozens of publicly traded companies. He is the largest private owner of farmland in the country, according to The Land Report. In addition to the Four Seasons, he has stakes in other luxury hotels and a company that caters to private jet owners. His real estate portfolio includes one of the largest houses in the country and several equestrian facilities. He owns stakes in a clean energy investment fund and a nuclear energy start-up.
但比爾·蓋茲幾十年來一直在多樣化他的資產。他僅擁有1.3％的微軟股份，但他的股票投資組合包括了數十家上市公司的股份。根據《土地報告》(The Land Report)，他是美國最大的農田私人擁有者。除了四季酒店外，他還擁有其他豪華酒店的股份以及一家專為私人飛機所有者服務的公司。他的房地產投資組合包括全國最大的豪宅之一和一些馬術設施。他擁有清潔能源投資基金和一家核能初創企業的股份。
There is also the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation. Separate from the rest of the Gates fortune, with an endowment of $50 billion, the foundation is one of the world’s largest charitable organizations and plays a uniquely important role in global public health. The endowment is in a trust and cannot be divided as part of the marital estate, though questions remain about whether it will be the main recipient of their charitable contributions once the split is complete.
還有比爾和梅琳達·蓋茲基金會(Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation)。該基金會與蓋茲的其他財富分開來，擁有500億美元的捐贈，是世界上最大的慈善組織之一，在全球公共衛生中發揮著無人能及的重要作用。捐贈是信託的，不能作為婚姻財產的一部分進行分拆。尚不清楚在財產分割後，基金會是否仍然是兩人慈善捐款的主要接受者。
The couple has a separation agreement in place, according to the divorce filing by Ms. French Gates, but the details are not disclosed. The filing asks the court to divide their real estate, personal property and debts according to the terms set forth in that agreement. Lawyers for Ms. French Gates have been working on a plan for separating some of the assets since 2019, a person with knowledge of the matter said.
根據弗蘭奇·蓋茲的離婚文件，這對夫妻已經簽署了分居協議，但未透露具體細節。該文件要求法院根據該協議中規定的條款劃分其房地產、個人財產和債務。一位知情人士說，自2019年以來，弗蘭奇·蓋茲的律師一直在制定一項分割部分資產的計劃。
Divorce lawyers not working on the Gates split say some of the personal assets could be hard to value, hard to separate and highly complex. Some of the wealth has already been divided: Soon after the announcement, $2.4 billion worth of shares in AutoNation, Canadian National Railway and two Mexican companies belonging to the couple were transferred to Ms. French Gates — making her a billionaire in her own right.
未參與蓋茲離婚案的離婚律師表示，某些個人資產可能難以估價，難以分割且非常複雜。其中一些財富已經被分割了：宣布離婚後不久，價值24億美元的AutoNation、加拿大國家鐵路公司(Canadian National Railway)和兩家屬於這對夫婦的墨西哥公司的股份被轉移給了弗蘭奇·蓋茲，這使她成為了億萬富翁。
An itemized list might be harder to come by.
具體的資產列表可能更難獲得。
廣告
“Divorces are actually one of the times that things crack open and the light shines in,” said Chuck Collins, senior scholar at the Institute for Policy Studies in Washington and author of “The Wealth Hoarders: How Billionaires Pay Millions to Hide Trillions.” But he added that prenuptial agreements and settlements were designed for privacy. “Parts of a prenup are about nondisclosure about all the family trusts and things,” he said. “They’ve got that all lined up before you fall in love.”
華盛頓政策研究所(Institute for Policy Studies)高級學者、《財富囤積者：億萬富翁如何花百萬美元來掩藏萬億美元》(The Wealth Hoarders: How Billionaires Pay Millions to Hide Trillions)一書的作者查克·科林斯(Chuck Collins)表示：「離婚實際上是撥雲見日的時候。」但是他還說，婚前協議及和解的初衷是保護隱私。「婚前協議的一部分是關於所有家庭信託和事情的保密協議，」他說。「在你墜入愛河之前，他們就已經準備好了。」
At the same time, lawyers point out that the issues that cause friction in the average divorce are completely absent for the stratospherically wealthy.
同時，律師指出，首富離婚完全沒有普通人離婚時會造成摩擦的那些問題。
“It’s almost easier to settle a case like this, if the parties are inclined to do so, than it is to settle a case where people live comfortably, but don’t have enough to live comfortably once they split everything in half,” Mr. Aronson said. “For these people, it will only change to the extent of how many billions of dollars they can give away to whatever they want.”
「兩個過著舒適的生活的人一旦將所有財產平分，就沒有足夠的錢繼續過得舒適，和解這樣的離婚案要更難，然而像這樣的離婚案，如果雙方都傾向於和解的話，和解起來更容易。」阿隆森說。「對於這些人來說，產生變化的只不過是他們捐獻給他們選擇的慈善機構的幾億美元的浮動。」
A Secretively Managed Fortune
祕密管理的財富
At the center of both the Gates fortune and the Gates Foundation endowment is a little-known entity called Cascade Investment. Based in Kirkland, Wash., and run by Michael Larson, a former bond fund manager for Putnam Investments, Cascade has overseen both the endowment and most of the personal wealth of Mr. Gates and Ms. French Gates for decades.
蓋茲的財富和蓋茲基金會兩者捐贈的核心均指向一個鮮為人知的名為瀑布投資(Cascade Investment)的實體。該公司總部位於華盛頓州的柯克蘭，由普特南投資公司(Putnam Investments)前債券基金經理麥可·拉爾森(Michael Larson)經營，數十年來，瀑布投資一直負責比爾·蓋茲和弗蘭奇·蓋茲的捐款和大部分個人財富。
Mr. Gates began reducing his stake in Microsoft beginning with the initial public offering in 1986, when he owned 45 percent of the company, a stake worth $350 million at the time. Today, he has an estimated net worth of $124 billion, according to Forbes, or $146 billion, according to the research firm Wealth-X. Including the Gates Foundation’s endowment and the Gates personal fortune, Cascade most likely oversees assets that put it on par or beyond some of the world’s biggest hedge funds in size.
比爾·蓋茲在1986年的首次公開募股時擁有公司45％的股份，當時的股份價值為3.5億美元。從那時他開始減持微軟股份。據《富比士》(Forbes)估計，如今他的凈資產為1240億美元，而根據研究公司Wealth-X的估計為1460億美元。蓋茲基金會的捐贈加上蓋茲的個人財富，瀑布投資負責的資產很可能達到了或超過了世界上某些最大對沖基金的資產。
Mr. Larson operates Cascade with an obsessive level of secrecy, going to great lengths to cloak the firm’s transactions so that they can’t easily be traced back to the Gateses. In a 1999 interview with Fortune magazine, Mr. Larson said he chose the name “Cascade” because it was a generic-sounding name in the Pacific Northwest.
拉爾森對瀑布的經營保密程度極高，竭盡全力掩蓋公司的交易，以免他人輕易追溯到蓋茲。拉爾森在1999年接受《財富》雜誌採訪時說，他之所以選擇「瀑布」，是因為這個名字在太平洋西北地區聽上去非常普通。
Mr. Larson’s wealth-management strategy is grounded in value investing — a long-term approach to finding solid, underpriced stocks. This approach is often associated with Warren E. Buffett, who is a close friend of Mr. Gates. Mr. Larson focuses on buying and holding brick-and-mortar companies rather than high-growth technology stocks. (Mr. Gates chooses his tech investments and holds them outside Cascade.)
拉爾森的財富管理策略以價值投資為基礎——尋找紮實、低價股票的長線做法。這種方法通常與比爾·蓋茲的密友沃倫·巴菲特(Warren E. Buffett)有關。拉爾森專注於購買和持有實體公司，而不是高增長的科技股。（比爾·蓋茲的科技投資由他自己選擇，並在瀑布投資之外持有。)
That strategy pays off for foundations and family wealth, both of which tend to focus on wealth preservation rather than making risky bets.
該策略為基金會和家庭財富帶來了回報，兩者都傾向於將重點放在財富保全上，而不是冒險下注。
“He has delivered the best possible outcome for decades, with absolute discretion,” said Roger McNamee, a Silicon Valley investor who helped found the private-equity firm Elevation Partners and has worked with Mr. Larson in the past.
矽谷投資者羅傑·麥克納姆(Roger McNamee)說：「數十年來，他絕對謹慎行事，獲得了最好的結果。」麥克納姆曾幫助創立私募股權公司Elevation Partners，並曾與拉爾森有過合作。
Mr. Larson has a particular fondness for luxury hotels, betting that top properties withstand recessions better than budget lodgings. Cascade owns various Four Seasons hotels, including one in Whistler, Canada, as well as the Charles Hotel in Cambridge, Mass.
拉爾森對豪華酒店情有獨鍾，他認為頂級酒店比經濟型旅館更能承受衰退。瀑布投資擁有許多家四季酒店，其中包括加拿大惠斯勒的一家，以及麻薩諸塞州劍橋的查爾斯酒店(Charles Hotel)。
In 2007, Mr. Gates teamed up to buy the Four Seasons hotel management company with Prince Alwaleed bin Talal for $3.8 billion, with each taking a 47.5 percent stake. Although Cascade has said it is committed to Four Seasons for the long haul, in recent years both Cascade and Kingdom Holding, Prince Alwaleed’s family office, have flirted with the idea of exiting at least part of their investment, according to a person familiar with their thinking.
2007年，比爾·蓋茲與阿爾瓦利德·本·塔拉勒親王(Prince Alwaleed bin Talal)聯手以38億美元的價格收購了四季酒店管理公司，兩人分別持股47.5％。根據一位了解兩人想法的人士說，儘管瀑布公司表示將長期持有四季酒店，但近年來，瀑布和阿爾瓦利德親王家族辦公室的王國控股公司都有過退出至少部分投資的想法。
廣告
Late in 2019, Mr. Gates and Prince Alwaleed discussed the possibility of an initial public offering of stock at a valuation of $10 billion, the person said. The idea of an I.P.O. or a private sale of one or both stakes has been broached again in recent months, the person added.
該知情人士說，2019年末，蓋茲和阿爾瓦利德親王曾討論首次公開募股的可能性，估值為100億美元。此人說，近幾個月，IPO或私下出售兩人或其中一人股份的想法再次被提起。
Kingdom officials did not respond to requests for comment. A Cascade spokesman declined to comment.
王國官員沒有回應置評請求。一名瀑布公司發言人拒絕置評。
The Future of Their Philanthropy
慈善事業的未來
Mr. Gates and Ms. French Gates have played such an outsize role in philanthropy that questions about the future of the Gates Foundation immediately arose following news of the divorce. The foundation directs billions to 135 countries to help fight poverty and disease. As of 2019, it had given away nearly $55 billion. (In 2006, Mr. Buffett pledged $31 billion of his fortune to the Gates Foundation, greatly increasing its grant making.)
蓋茲和弗蘭奇·蓋茲的慈善事業舉足輕重，因此在兩人離婚的消息傳出後，人們立即開始就蓋茲基金會的未來提出疑問。基金會向135個國家提供數百億美元的資金用於應對貧困和疾病問題。截至2019年，基金會已撥出近550億美元。（2006年，巴菲特將310億美元個人財富捐給蓋茲基金會，經費撥發規模因此大幅增加。）
Since he stepped down from day-to-day operations at Microsoft in 2008, Mr. Gates has devoted much of his time to the foundation. He also runs Gates Ventures, a firm that invests in companies working on climate change and other issues. Over the decades, Mr. Gates shed the image of a ruthless tech executive battling the United States government on antitrust to be viewed as a global do-gooder. And he appears to be keenly aware of the stark contrast between the scale of his wealth and his role as a philanthropist. “I’ve been disproportionately rewarded for the work I’ve done — while many others who work just as hard struggle to get by,” he acknowledged in a year-end blog post from 2019.
自2008年不再參與微軟公司日常運營以來，蓋茲將大量時間用於基金會的工作。他還運營了蓋茲創投(Gates Ventures)，專門向從事氣候變化和其他問題應對的公司投資。幾十年來，當年作為企業高管與美國政府在反壟斷問題上拚死對抗的蓋茲，已經在全球建立起善人形象。他似乎很清楚自己的財富規模與慈善家身份之間存在劇烈反差。「我的工作得到了不相稱的高額回報——而很多人和我一樣努力，卻只能勉強維持，」他在2019年的一篇年末博文中承認。
The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation was created in 2000. Today, the two serve as its co-chairs, which the foundation has said will not change after the divorce is complete.
比爾和梅琳達·蓋茲基金會創辦於2000年。兩人至今仍是基金會的聯席主席，基金會表示這一點在離婚程序完成後也不會改變。
But Ms. French Gates has recently been more public about the efforts of her other group, Pivotal Ventures, which is focused on gender equality and social progress. It remains unclear what level of resources will be at her disposal when the divorce is complete, but Ms. French Gates will most likely still wield enormous influence in the world of philanthropy.
但弗蘭奇·蓋茲近年來會更多談及她的另一個企業樞紐創投(Pivotal Ventures)，專注於性別平等和社會進步。目前尚不清楚在兩人離婚後她將掌握怎樣的資源，但可以想像弗蘭奇·蓋茲仍將是慈善界的重量級人物。
廣告
“There’s no explanation how you get to be in this situation of privilege,” Ms. French Gates told The New York Times last year. “There’s just none.”
「一個人怎麼就可以身居如此優越的地位，」弗蘭奇·蓋茲去年接受時報採訪時說。「我完全想不出該如何解釋。」
