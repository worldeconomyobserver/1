日本經濟第三季度萎縮0.9%

In Reversal of Earlier Quarters, Data Suggest Japan Is Near Recession

HIROKO TABUCHI
2012年11月13日
TOKYO — Japan’s economy shrank at a pace of 0.9 percent in the three months through September, government data showed Monday, as sluggish imports compounded slowing demand and edged the nation’s economy toward recession.
東京——根據日本政府周一發佈的數據，截止9月的第三季度，日本經濟以0.9%的速度萎縮，低迷的進口使需求放緩更加嚴重，將該國逼到了衰退邊緣。
The fall in gross domestic product came to an annualized 3.5 percent contraction. It was a reversal from Japan’s robust performance earlier this year, when it outperformed most of its peers in the Group of 7 industrialized nations.
國內生產總值的年化收縮比率為3.5%。這一數字逆轉了日本經濟今年早些時候的強健表現，當時日本的表現比七國集團(G7)中的大多數國家都要好。
But growth has since stalled, hurt by a slump in exports amid economic woes in Europe and a damaging territorial spat with China, both major trading destinations for Japan. A strong yen has also hurt competitiveness among Japanese exporters.
但是，自那時以來，增長已經停滯，原因是歐洲經濟災難帶來的低迷出口，以及中日領土爭端造成的損害，歐洲和中國都是日本的主要貿易夥伴。日元走強也削弱了日本出口商的競爭力。
At home, the pace of reconstruction after last year’s destructive earthquake, tsunami and nuclear disaster appears to have slowed, with private consumption declining at the largest rate since early 2011.
在國內，去年的毀滅性地震、海嘯和核災難之後的重建工作似乎已經放緩，個人消費降速為2011年初以來最大。
廣告
Economists expect the Japanese economy to continue to shrink in the next quarter, pitching it into recession before conditions improve.
經濟學家預計，日本經濟將在接下來的這個季度繼續萎縮，退回經濟改善之前的衰退狀態。
“Although the Japanese economy will face harsh challenges for the time being, the situation is likely to bottom out by the end of the year,” economists at the Mizuho Research Institute in Tokyo said in a note after the economic numbers were released.
“儘管日本經濟將暫時面臨嚴峻挑戰，經濟形勢仍有可能在今年年底觸底反彈，”經濟數據公布之後，東京瑞穗研究所(Mizuho Research Institute)的經濟學家在一份報告中說。
“A rebound in economic indicators in the United States and China suggests that Japan’s exports will also bottom out by the year’s end, so the possibility that the economy will continue to retrench in the new year is low. However, there is a risk that if Japan’s relations with China worsen and exacerbate the negative effects on trade, recovery could take longer,” the economists said.
“美國和中國經濟指標的反彈表明，日本的出口也將在今年年底觸底反彈，因此，經濟在新的一年裡持續收縮的可能性不大。然而，目前仍有一個風險，那就是如果日本與中國的關係繼續惡化，加大貿易承受的負面效應，復蘇就可能需要更長的時間，”前述經濟學家說。
The numbers put renewed pressure on Japan’s central bank to do more to shore up the economy. Last month, the Bank of Japan eased monetary policy for the second month in a row by expanding an asset lending program, after government officials urged the bank to take more drastic steps to keep economic recovery on track.
這些數字帶來了新的壓力，迫使日本央行採取更多提振經濟的措施。在政府官員督促日本銀行(Bank of Japan)採取更積極的措施來維持經濟復蘇勢頭之後，上個月，日本銀行擴大了資產貸款計劃，連續兩個月採取了放寬貨幣政策的措施。
The bank is set to review monetary policy again next week.
日本銀行將在下周再次評估其貨幣政策。
A mature economy with a declining population, Japan, now the world’s third-largest economy behind the United States and China, is being eclipsed by its Asian neighbors. In a report published last week on long-term economic trends, the Organization of Economic Cooperation and Development said Japan would continue to grow at a sluggish rate, its share of the global economy falling far behind that of fast-growing China and India.
隨着經濟的成熟和人口的下降，日本這個名列美國和中國之後的世界第三大經濟體，已經因為亞洲鄰國的紛紛崛起而顯得黯然失色。在上周發佈的一份關於長期經濟趨勢的報告中，經濟合作與發展組織(Organization of Economic Cooperation and Development)稱，日本經濟將繼續低速增長，在全球經濟中所佔份額將遠遠地落後於迅速增長的中國和印度。
