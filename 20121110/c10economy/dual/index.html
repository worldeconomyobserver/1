由借贷推动的中国经济复苏

Fueled by Debt, a Recovery Takes Hold in China

KEITH BRADSHER
2012年11月10日

BEIJING – With construction cranes moving again from Guangzhou to Beijing and with steel mills and concrete factories busy once more, the Chinese economy is showing signs of a debt-fueled recovery this autumn even as the United States and particularly the European Union continue to struggle.

北京——今年秋天，正当美国、尤其是欧洲经济仍步履维艰之际，随着从广州到北京中国各个工地上的起重机重新开动、钢厂和混凝土工厂再度忙碌起来，中国经济正表现由出借贷推动的复苏迹象。

Industrial production, fixed-asset investment, retail sales and electricity generation all strengthened more than expected last month, continuing a trend that began in September, while inflation slowed more than expected. State-owned banks have released a torrent of loans to state-owned enterprises since May, producing a swift revival of investment spending this autumn but also raising questions about the efficiency of those investments.

上个月，中国的工业产出、固定资产投资、零售业销售额以及电产量等各个指标的表现都超出了预期，延续了9月份的趋势，与此同时，通货膨胀率的下降幅度也大过预期。自5月份以来，国有银行已经向国有企业发放了大量贷款，使得投资性支出比例在秋季迅速反弹，但同时也引发了对这些投资的效率的质疑。

Friday’s data came out as the country’s senior officials and top military officers have gathered here this week for a once-a-decade leadership transition at the Party Congress, prompting some skepticism overseas and in China that the data might have been manipulated for political benefit.

周五数据公开时，国家高层官员以及军队高层领导人正在参加本周召开的共产党全国代表大会，进行十年一次的权力交接，这使得中国国内和海外的一些人怀疑，政府可能为了政治目的，操纵了数据。

But while many economists and business executives say that Chinese economic data MAY appear to have been deliberately altered over the spring and summer so as to hide the severity of an economic slowdown, they expressed more confidence that the Chinese economy is now on the mend. An aging work force, overcapacity in many industries and very heavy corporate debts appear to be producing a weaker recovery than in 2010, however, with little sign that the encouraging economic indicators released on Friday point to growth rates that will reach double digits again anytime soon.

但是，尽管许多经济学家和工商业高管说中国的经济数据也许看来在今年春天和夏天被有意改动过，以掩盖当时经济增速放缓的严重形势，但他们对中国经济现在的好转表示了更多的信心。然而，老龄化的劳动人口、多个行业内的产能过剩以及超大规模的公司债务好像都使这次复苏不如2010年时强劲，而且周五发布的经济指标虽预示着好兆头，但也没有任何迹象表明经济增长率在近期内能再次达到双位数。

 “Given that they have been published while the Party Congress is in session, some sceptics have questioned whether they can be believed,” Capital Economics, a London consulting firm, said in a research note about Friday’s data. “In our view, there is solid evidence of a turnaround but not of a strong rebound.”

“考虑到数据发布时，中国正在召开党代会，一些人便怀疑它们的可信度，”位于伦敦的咨询公司凯投宏观(Capital Economics)在一项关于周五发布的数据的研究报告中写道。“我们认为，有足够证据表明，中国经济发展趋势出现了好转，但是没有强劲反弹。”

Zhou Xiaochuan, the governor of the People’s Bank of China, the country’s central bank, said at a press conference at the Party Congress on Thursday evening that the economy appeared to have turned a corner even before the strong performance in October.

周四傍晚，在十八大的一次新闻发布会上，中国央行中国人民银行行长周小川说，中国经济在10月份的强劲表现之前，就应该已经开始好转。

 “After China's economy moderated in the second quarter, domestic policies adjusted, helping the economy to stabilize in the third quarter, especially in September,” he said, predicting that the economy would continue to grow, unless lingering economic troubles overseas create renewed difficulties for China as well.

“中国经济在第二季度出现放缓迹象，经过一系列国内政策调整后，国内经济在今年第三季度特别是９月份出现缓中趋稳，”周小川说，并预测经济会持续增长，除非海外持续的经济麻烦给中国带来新的困难。

Many worries persist about the sustainability of even a modest recovery heavily reliant on debt. Chinese banks are lending at such a brisk pace that by the end of next year, they will have expanded their balance sheets in just five years by an amount equal to the combined balance sheets of the entire American commercial banking system, according to Fitch Ratings. Yet China’s economy is only half the size of the American economy.

人们的担忧依然存在，认为即使是不太明显的复苏，靠严重依赖债务是难以持续的。中国的银行正在发放贷款的速度如此之快，以至于惠誉公司(Fitch Ratings)估计，到明年年底，它们的资产负债在五年内的增幅将相当于美国整个商业银行体系资产负债的总和。但是，中国的经济规模却只有美国的一半。

Each extra dollar of lending since 2008 has produced less than half as much extra economic growth as before the global financial crisis. State-owned enterprises have finished urgent tasks like building enough steel mills to meet domestic demand and are now investing in less and less economically viable projects to sustain economic activity.

自2008年以来，中国的银行每增加一美元贷款所创造的经济增长不到金融危机之前同样一美元带来的经济增长的一半。国有企业已完成了急需的任务，比如建设足够多的钢厂以满足内需，现在开始投资于一些经济可行性越来越小的项目，来维持其商业运作。

As a result, the loan burden of China’s corporate sector is soaring relative to the country’s economic output, reaching 1.9 times economic output this year after staying steady at around 1.2 times economic output in the years before the global financial crisis.

结果导致中国企业部门的债务负担，与国家的经济产出相比急剧上升，今年达到经济产出的1.9倍，而在全球金融危机之前的几年中，企业负债一直较稳定地保持在经济产出的1.2倍左右。

Charlene Chu, the head of Chinese bank ratings at Fitch, predicted that such heavy Chinese lending could not continue indefinitely. "Rising leverage either will swamp borrowers' ability to repay, or banks' funding and capital needs will fall short of existing resources," she said.

惠誉公司中国银行评级部门的负责人朱夏莲(Charlene Chu)预测称，中国如此庞大的贷款规模不可能无止境地持续下去。“债务杠杆的不断上升或者会使借款人无力偿贷，或者现有资源将无法满足银行的融资和资金需要。”

Shang Fulin, the chairman of the China Banking Regulatory Commission, joined Mr. Zhou at the press conference on Thursday evening and said that regulators had made sure that banks were not further increasing their lending to local governments, but he did not directly address corporate lending. Mr. Zhou, Mr. Shang and the chairmen of the country’s Big Four banks have scheduled for Sunday evening their first joint press conference in memory, to discuss the health of the financial system.

周四傍晚，中国银监会主席尚福林和周小川一起出席了新闻发布会，他表示，监管部门已确保银行不会进一步增加给地方政府的贷款，但是他并没有直接回应企业贷款的问题。周小川、尚福林和中国四大国有银行行长已计划将于周日晚召开人们记忆中的第一次联合会议，讨论中国金融系统的健康问题。

The Chinese economy is especially dependent on investment spending these days because exports have not resumed the rapid growth to which many companies had become accustomed. Commerce Minister Chen Deming said at a press conference on Friday afternoon at the Party Congress that China’s exports would fall short of the government’s target of around 10 percent growth this year, after rising by a little over 6 percent so far this year.

近来，由于出口一直没有恢复到很多公司已习惯的快速增长，中国经济对投资性支出的依赖程度变得更为明显。商务部部长陈德铭在周五下午召开的十八大记者招待会上表示，今年，中国的出口额增长没有达到政府制定的约10%的目标，目前为止只增长了6%多一点。

The timing of abrupt zigzags in Chinese economic policy appears to have matched factional struggles within the Communist Party, although Chinese officials have not acknowledged any connection.

尽管中国领导人尚未承认两者之间的任何关系，中国经济政策出现突然波动的时间看起来与共产党内部的派系斗争同步。
