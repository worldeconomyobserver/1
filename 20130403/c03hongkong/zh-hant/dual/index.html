鄭少秋新片又成港股不祥之兆

For Hong Kong Markets, Bad Omen in a Movie Premiere

GERRY MULLANY
2013年4月3日
HONG KONG — There are all sorts of reasons for the Hong Kong stock market to have the jitters these days. The Chinese economy remains wobbly. North Korea is threatening nuclear war. And perhaps most worrisome this week, a leading Hong Kong actor has a new movie coming out.
香港——近期，香港股市有各種理由出現震蕩：中國經濟仍不穩定；朝鮮威脅發動核戰爭。此外，本周最令人擔心的因素可能是，香港的一名主要男演員有新電影上線。
Hong Kong investors call it the Ting Hai effect: When the actor Adam Cheng has a new television show or a new movie, the Hang Seng index often takes a dramatic turn for the worse. And in a city where superstitions can guide daily routines, the financial sector is worried.
香港投資者稱此為丁蟹效應：每逢演員鄭少秋( Adam Cheng)的新電視劇或新電影上映，恒生指數常常急劇下跌。香港是一個迷信可以影響日常生活的城市，這裡的金融業很是擔心。
Mr. Cheng’s new movie, “Saving General Yang,” has its debut in theaters Wednesday, and investors in the Hong Kong market, which has been generally buoyant of late, are on guard, while acknowledging that it is unlikely that a movie opening could drive a major market index.
周三，鄭少秋新片《忠烈楊家將》首映，有鑒於此，儘管香港股市近期形勢大好，投資者卻滿心警惕，儘管他們也承認，一部影片的上線不太可能影響一個主要股指。
“Of course, investors need to be wary to an extent,” said Thomas Wong, a Hong Kong banker. “That is part of the job, but they shouldn’t let a movie decide whether the stock market will do well or not. That is not how the market works. People may talk about it, but they are not going to take it seriously.”
香港銀行家托馬斯·黃(Thomas Wong)說，“當然，投資者們需要保持一定程度的警惕。警惕本來就是投資行為的一部分，但他們不能讓一部電影來決定股市的漲跌。市場不是這麼運作的。人們可能會談論這件事情，但卻不會當真。”
The Ting Hai effect has its roots in the 1992 debut of a television series called “The Greed of Man,” which turned a critical eye on members of the city’s investment community and their supposed shady doings, with Mr. Cheng in the role of Ting Hai, who made it rich selling derivatives during a bear market. As the first episode was being broadcast, the Hang Seng dropped an alarming 13 percent at one point.
丁蟹效應源於1992年電視連續劇《大時代》的開播。該劇批判地審視香港的投資者群體，以及他們那些據說見不得光的交易。鄭少秋在劇中飾演丁蟹，此人憑藉在熊市中拋售金融衍生產品而致富。第一集播出時，恒生指數一度令人揪心地暴跌了13%。
Investor concerns about his acting appearances were reinforced two years later when the market took a drop of nearly 14 percent with the debut of his television series “Instinct.”
兩年後，鄭少秋出演的《笑看風雲》開播，股市下跌近14%。投資者對鄭少秋出鏡的擔憂由此得到了強化。
The unpredictability of financial markets has long fed superstitions about stock market behavior. One school of thought suggests that market downturns coincide with the construction of the world’s tallest building: Think the Empire State Building in New York in 1931; the Sears Tower in Chicago in 1974; and the Petronas Twin Towers in Kuala Lumpur in 1998. And until recent years, there was a notable correlation between bear markets and victories by teams from the American Football Conference in the Super Bowl.
金融市場的不可預測性早已引發了關於股市動態的迷信。根據一種說法，熊市總是會出現在世界最高建築落成之時，比如1931年在紐約落成的帝國大廈，1974年在芝加哥落成的西爾斯大廈，以及1998年在吉隆坡落成的石油雙塔。此外，直到近幾年，熊市一直與美國橄欖球聯合會(American Football Conference)旗下球隊奪得超級碗(Super Bowl)的事件存在明顯的對應關係。
In Mr. Cheng’s case, the market has not always become agitated when he starts a new role: in 6 of his 17 television program debuts, the Hang Seng rose, according to The South China Morning Post. But that has not allayed the fears of investors, and now Mr. Cheng has another new movie. “Saving General Yang” has the actor in the lead role of a drama about a general in the pre-derivatives Song Dynasty, 1,100 years ago.
就鄭少秋的例子而言，股市並不總是在他開演新角色時出現動蕩。據《南華早報》(South China Morning Post)稱，他主演過17部電視劇集，其中6部開播時，恒生指數出現上揚。但這並沒有消除投資者的恐懼，而眼下，鄭少秋又參演了一部新電影。鄭少秋在《忠烈楊家將》中飾演主角，是一位將軍，影片故事來自1100年前的宋朝。
“Hong Kong investors can be very superstitious,” Francis Cheung, an analyst at the brokerage firm CLSA, wrote about the Ting Hai phenomenon. “And Adam Cheng is a very well-known actor with a large following.”
“香港投資者有時會非常迷信，”證券公司里昂證券(CLSA)的分析師鄭名凱(Francis Cheung)就丁蟹效應寫道。“而鄭少秋又是一位非常出名的演員，有一大批影迷。”
Investors spooked by superstitions already have reason to worry. This is the Year of the Snake, and four of the past five snake years, beginning with 1953, produced down markets, according to the financial services group CLSA, which publishes a tongue-in-cheek “Feng Shui Index.”
被迷信糾纏的投資者已經有了擔心的理由。據金融服務服務機構里昂證券稱，今年是蛇年，而在從1953年算起的過去五個蛇年中，有四個蛇年股市都下跌了。里昂證券發佈了一個半開玩笑的“風水指數”。
Snakes are skin shedders, whose years are associated with major transformations and change, like the Tiananmen Square crackdown, bombing of Pearl Harbor and the onset of the Great Depression.
蛇會蛻皮，蛇年讓人想到一些重大事變，比如天安門事件、珍珠港事件以及大蕭條的開始。
A CLSA report on the Ting Hai effect — again, tongue in cheek — found that the more tragic the story line in an Adam Cheng movie, the worse the impact for the market. While the details of the plot of “Saving General Yang” have not been revealed, in history, the general met a bad fate — dying of starvation after being captured in battle.
里昂證券就丁蟹效應發佈了一份同樣半開玩笑的報告，報告發現，鄭少秋參演的電影情節越悲慘，股市遭受的影響就越嚴重。儘管《忠烈楊家將》的具體情節尚未披露，但從歷史上看，這位將軍遭遇了不測，先是在戰鬥中被俘，後來又絕食而死。
